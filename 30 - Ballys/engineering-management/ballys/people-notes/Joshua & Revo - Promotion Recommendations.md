## Joshua

**Recommendation:** Promotion to Mid-Level Engineer

### Summary

Joshua is the strongest junior engineer on the team and performs on par with — if not above — several of our mid-level developers. He delivers with senior-level speed and quality, owns his work end-to-end, and consistently absorbs coaching without needing constant supervision.

### Key Strengths

- **Self-directed ownership:** When assigned a project, he takes full accountability and covers all bases without micromanagement.
- **Fast, high-quality delivery:** Despite his junior title, his output rivals that of senior developers.
- **Coachable and growing:** He seeks and applies feedback proactively. Occasional guidance is needed, but never hand-holding.
- **Extreme potential:** His trajectory suggests he will outgrow the mid-level band quickly if given the opportunity.

### Notable Contributions

| Project | Role / Impact |
|---------|---------------|
| UK — Multi-language | Core implementation |
| UK — Monopoly Rebrand | Core implementation |
| NA — Cookie Settings & GPC (omni-host & albatross) | Delivered end-to-end |
| NA — Albatross Migration | Owned and delivered |
| Side Project — Playwright POC | **Spearheaded** the dev work; first in Manila to create end-to-end tests and integrate with TestRail, partnering with QE |

### Verdict

Joshua has more than earned the mid-level title. This recommendation is for both retention and recognition of abilities.
