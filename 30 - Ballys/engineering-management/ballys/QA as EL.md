•	**Planning and Team Management:** The ability to plan effectively, manage people, and keep the team cohesive.
•	**Decision-Making:** Someone who can ask the tough questions, make smart decisions, and isn’t afraid to push back when necessary.
•	**Stakeholder Management:** Balancing stakeholder expectations while ensuring high-quality outcomes.
•	**Leadership and Team Dynamics:** Building strong relationships within the team, setting clear goals, and guiding the team toward achieving them.
•	**Technical Understanding:** While they don’t need to be a technical expert, they should understand the landscape well enough to lead the team in solving issues.
•	**Commitment to Growth:** Encouraging continuous learning and professional development within the team.
