# How To Build Your App Onboarding Like A Pro ($40k/Month) - PrayerLock Founder

Mobile app onboarding strategy using a three-act story structure.

## What It Is

A YouTube video breaking down how PrayerLock structures their app onboarding to maximize conversion, using a narrative framework.

## Key Takeaways

- **Three-act structure:** Intro / Climax / Conclusion applied to onboarding screens
- **Problem/Solution framing** in the first 3 screens
- **10-15 minute onboarding** creates an "investment effect" — users who invest time are more likely to convert
- **Trial experience during onboarding** — let users feel value before asking for money
- **Upfront pricing** — show pricing early to filter serious users
- **Target 10% download-to-trial conversion** before adding more features

## Why It Matters

Directly applicable to [[YouPastor-PRD|YouPastor]] onboarding design. Desktop apps still need strong first-run experiences.
