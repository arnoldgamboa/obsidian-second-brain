**God Has Set Limits and Provided Escape Routes**

1 Corinthians 10:13 (CSB)
No temptation has come upon you except what is common to humanity. But God is faithful; he will not allow you to be tempted beyond what you are able, but with the temptation he will also provide the way out so that you may be able to bear it.

> Every single time you're tempted, there's a way out. Every. Single. Time. 
> The Greek word for "way out" is *ekbasis* - it literally means an exit ramp. God doesn't just throw you into spiritual battle and hope you figure it out. He provides specific, practical exits from every tempting situation.


> ### **How does God provides  temptation exit routes?**

**Identify your temptations**
What 
Who  
When 

**What** - What specific temptations do you face? Lust, anger, greed, pride, fear? Name them specifically, not generally.

**Who** - Who are you with when you're most vulnerable? Are there certain people who make it easier for you to compromise? Or are you most vulnerable when you're alone?

**When** - When are you most susceptible? Late at night? When you're stressed? After a difficult day? When you're celebrating? Identify your high-risk times.

Spiritual x-ray
0 to 4
1. Do you feel more physically exhausted or energetic
2. Are you more discouraged/pesimistic or encouraged/hopeful
3. Are you more bored/discontented or challenged/contented
4. Are you more spiritually dry/empty or alive/growing
5. Are you feeling more alone/distant or close tothose you love
6. Do you feel more insecure or confident
7. Are you feeling wounded/hurt or love/understood
8. Bitter/angry or no regrets/resentment
9. Sad or happy
10. Failed or succeeding 

30 to 40 - in good shape, resistant to temptation
20 to 30 - in serious danger, yellow lights, be aware of satan’s temptations
