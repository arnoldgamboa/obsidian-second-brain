**Practical Steps:**

1. **REPENT of sins**

**True Repentance Means:**
- Agreeing with God about your sin
- Turning away from sinful patterns
- Not just feeling sorry, but choosing change
**Memorable Line:** *"Repentance isn't just feeling bad about sin - it's feeling bad enough to stop."*

**3. RESOLVE to Make Restitution (Where Possible)**

**This Doesn't Mean:**
- You earn forgiveness through restitution
- Every wrong can be made right
- You're responsible for others' responses
**This Does Mean:**
- Taking responsibility for your actions
- Making amends where appropriate
- Demonstrating genuine change
