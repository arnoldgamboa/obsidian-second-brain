“Pano Nga Ba Mabuhay Forever?”
“Heaven is Not What You Think”

Luke 23:39-43 (CSB)
Then one of the criminals hanging there began to yell insults at him: “Aren’t you the Messiah? Save yourself and us! ”

Salvation is not as easy as it seems.
- Criminal is just an arms away from Jesus but still misses the point of what he has done.
- Child of a pastor, grew up in church, been to church all your life, but still misses it
- Jesus is just a tool for this criminal. “Save yourself and us!”

Luke 23:39-43 (CSB)
But the other answered, rebuking him: “Don’t you even fear God, since you are undergoing the same punishment? We are punished justly, because we’re getting back what we deserve for the things we did, but this man has done nothing wrong.” 
- Guy is a sinner, he knows it, admits it
- Knows they deserve

The reason why salvation is not as easy as it seems is because it requires admission of your incapacity to save yourself. 

Luke 23:39-43 (CSB)
Jesus, remember me , when you come into your kingdom.”
- Personal, called Jesus by his name
- Remember me, requires faith
- Didn’t ask to fix his current situation, he looks to eternity with Him instead

Luke 23:39-43 (CSB)
And he said to him, “Truly I tell you, today you will be with me in paradise.” '
- Promise is “today”, no waiting, no purgatory
- Criminal no chance to do other good works, just trust.
- With me, Christ’s presence
- “Paradise”, Heaven is not what you think.

Salvation is not easy but it is simple. Trust in Jesus’ alone for his promise of eternal life.

Have you trusted in Jesus’ alone and in his promise of eternal life?
