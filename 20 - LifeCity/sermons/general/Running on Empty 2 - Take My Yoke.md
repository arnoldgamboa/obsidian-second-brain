“Take my yoke upon you…”

- What is a yoke. (Not the yellow part of an egg)
- Piece of wood that brings animal farm together so load is lighter
- It’s not a harness. 
- Yoke is teamed up two or more together so it’ll be easier on the animal 

Yoke is a symbol of partnership

- &nbsp;
	- Jesus is saying, let me partner with you.
	- Team up with me, I will help you carry the load

If you’re feeling stressed right now, it could be that you’re not yoked with Jesus.
Stress is a warning light.
- You maybe a believer, but if you’re stressed out, it could be you’re not connected with Jesus.

Yoke is a symbol of control.

- &nbsp;
	- Farmer holds it, you’re with another person/animal. You go with where the other animal goes
	- We get into trouble when we’re not yoked with Jesus.
		- We go too fast, we go too slow, or we go in a different direction, fall off the ditch.
		- If you’re connected to Jesus, he knows exactly how to go with life, because he created you.


Everyone is yoked to something else.
- You’re yoked to your job
- You’re yoked to making money
- You’re yoked to your success

- Jesus as carpenter might have created yokes
- He created perfectly fit yokes
- His yoke fits perfectly on you

God knows you. It’s easier to go about life when you do exactly what God has created you to be.
