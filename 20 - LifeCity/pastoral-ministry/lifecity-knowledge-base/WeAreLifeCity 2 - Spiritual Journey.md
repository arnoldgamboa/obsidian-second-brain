**God meets you where you are, then grows you to where he wants you to be.**

“God loves you just the way you are, but He refuses to leave you that way. He wants you to be just like Jesus. ” - Max Lucado
 

**When it comes to spiritual growth, we think “next steps” not “stages”**
- Not classes, not how much you know about the Bible 
- Everyone needs to grow into the likeness of Jesus. Nobody is exempted.  
- New Christians, seasoned Christians, everyone is in a journey towards Christlikeness 

**What’s your next step?**
- LifeGroups (relationships)? 
- Learn the Bible (Sunday Experiences) 
- Generousity (Tithing) 
- Baptism 
- Mentorships 


My dream:
- a church that accept everyone who ever they are, where ever they’ve been, what ever they’ve done 
- a church passionate about mentoring each one 
- a church filled with people taking steps as they grow in their relationship with Jesus 
- a church that is haven for people to come as they are, not judged, and take their next step into growing
