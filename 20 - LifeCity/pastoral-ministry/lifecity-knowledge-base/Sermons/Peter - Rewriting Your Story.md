1. IS THE BOTTOM LINE CLEAR?
MY BOTTOM LINE: Your relationship with God is not defined by your successes or failures. Your relationship with God is determined by your identity in Christ.


2. WHAT’S MY OPENING LINE?
MY OPENING LINE: Quick survey: Raise hand, how long have you been a Christian? 5 years? 10 years? 20 years? Me, early 2000s.

3. INTRODUCTION
MY TRANSITION: One of the privileges of being a pastor for years is to see how people grow in their relationship with Christ. And also failures. Today, we see this same story.
    
4. WHAT’S MY MAIN POINT?
MY MAIN POINT: You are not your failures. You are who God  says you are.

5. DOES IT MATTER?
WHY IT MATTERS: Christian life can be discouraging at times. But you need to focus on the objective facts, not on subjective feelings.

6. WHERE’S THE POWER IN THE TEXT?
POWER IN THE TEXT: Luke 22:61 - At that moment the Lord turned and looked at Peter.
Main text: Luke 22:54-64

7. HOW AM I MOVING TOWARD APPLICATION?
THE APPLICATION:
Do you know what suffering of Jesus is about for Peter — and for you?
He is rewriting your story. 
He is carrying you through.
He is working for your good.
He is making you beginnings.
He is looking at you with love.

Romans 8:35-38 NIV
Who shall separate us from the love of Christ? Shall trouble or hardship or persecution or famine or nakedness or danger or sword? …
“No, in all these things we are more than conquerors through him who loved us. For I am convinced that neither death nor life, neither angels nor demons, neither the present nor the future, nor any powers, neither height nor depth, nor anything else in all creation, will be able to separate us from the love of God that is in Christ Jesus our Lord.

The resurrection changes everything.
     
8. WHAT’S MY MAIN TO DO (application)?
THE TO-DO:  His finished work is your fresh start.

9. WHY DOES IT MATTER?
THE WHY:  In times when you’re “weeping bitterly” for what you’ve done,  Jesus was just looking at you with love. Remember, you might have betrayed him at that bonfire, but because of Easter, Jesus is waiting, preparing that  bonfire breakfast for you.

10. HOW AM I CLOSING?
My Closing: Here’s the message of the gospel — “Your failure is not your identity. Your identity is who you are in Christ.”
