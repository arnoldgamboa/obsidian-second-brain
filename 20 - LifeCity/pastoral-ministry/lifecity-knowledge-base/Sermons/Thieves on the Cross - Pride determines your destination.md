Sin of all sin — pride.

Pride is excessive love for one’s own excellence.

Pride is the one sin that’s easiest to see in others but incredibly hard to see in ourselves.

Pride is refusing to trust God.

It’s not weakness to admit sinfulness. 
It’s not weakness to depend on someone stronger.

In order to be saved, you first need to acknowledge your need for a savior. 
This is where pride will keep you from heaven.

- our own self righteousness or Christ’s righteousness
