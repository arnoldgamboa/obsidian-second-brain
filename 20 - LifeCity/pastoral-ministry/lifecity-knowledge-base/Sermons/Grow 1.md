Stages of Salvation

Stages of Christian Experience 
Justification  - Jesus as your Savior; one time event; rebirth
Sanctification - Jesus as your Lord: continuous daily event: growing 
Glorification - Jesus as your King: Future event: glorified body 

Most Christians got stuck in the sanctification/growth phase. 
- Immature 
- Temptations 
- Problems
- Attitudes 

A growing follower of Jesus is someone who is intentionally deepening their relationship with God, continuously becoming more like Christ in their character and actions, and actively participating in God's mission to love and redeem the world.

Intentional. Continuous. Active.
