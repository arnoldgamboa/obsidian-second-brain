- Have you noticed — we love to receive mercy and grace but not be the one to be giving it?
- Case in point, traffic. I say sorry when I got the violation, but ticked off when someone cuts me off
- Can be as small as traffic violation, but it can go as profound as ‘forgiveness’
- It’s easier to recognize that you made the mistake and apologize but harder if we’re the one to be the one to be giving it

We want mercy for ourselves but justice for everybody else.
- human nature, that’s normal
- we protect ourselves 
- somebody did something wrong to us, we want justice to be served
- when we’re the one who gave
