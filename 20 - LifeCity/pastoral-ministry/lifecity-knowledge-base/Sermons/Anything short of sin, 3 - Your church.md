A Prayer for My Church, LifeCity

God, Thank You for placing LifeCity in my life who love You and care about me. I have been shaped by its wisdom, compassion, kindness, and selfless love.

Encourage my church when it feels worn out, and renew its strength when it is overwhelmed. Give rest to those who need it, and remind it of Your promises.

Bless the families, strengthen the ministries, and expand the influence of my church for Your glory, and its greater good.
