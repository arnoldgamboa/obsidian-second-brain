<span style="font-family:.AppleSystemUIFontBold;font-size:17.279998779296875pt;"><b>God Loves You This Much</b></span>
<span style="font-size:21.33333396911621pt;">The Bible says God is love. It doesn’t say God has love; it says God is love. Love is his nature. God is love.</span>
<span style="font-size:21.33333396911621pt;">God created the entire universe. He created this planet; he created the human race; then, he created you because he loves you.</span>
<span style="font-size:21.33333396911621pt;">God created you as an object of his love. He made you so he could love you and so you could love him. God’s love for you is the reason your heart is beating right now; it’s the reason you’re breathing.</span>
<span style="font-size:21.33333396911621pt;">God’s good news is that he loves you on your good days as much as he loves you on your bad days. He loves you when you can feel his love, and he loves you when you can’t seem to feel his love. He loves you whether or not you think you deserve his love.</span>
<span style="font-size:21.33333396911621pt;">There is nothing you can do that will make God stop loving you. You could try, but you simply can’t do it, because his love for you is based on his character and not on anything you do or say or feel.</span>
<span style="font-size:21.33333396911621pt;">God loves you so much that he sent his only Son, Jesus, into this world, “so that everyone who believes in him will not perish but have eternal life” (John 3:16 NLT, second edition).</span>
<span style="font-size:21.33333396911621pt;">Jesus stretched his arms out as wide as the cross, saying, “I love you this much! I love you so much it hurts. I love you so much I’ll die for you so that I won’t have to live without you.”</span>
<span style="font-size:21.33333396911621pt;">God’s love surpasses all human knowledge, and it is difficult for any of us to grasp the width and length and height and depth of his love for each one of us (Ephesians 3:17-19).</span>
