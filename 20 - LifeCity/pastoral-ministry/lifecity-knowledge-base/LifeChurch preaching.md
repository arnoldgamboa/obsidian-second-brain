Purpose:
- to teach the Bible — it’s doctrines, verse by verse — and make it come alive in the most relevant of city daily lives.

Goals:
- feed the intellect
- touch the emotions
- create desire for life change

“Human behavior grows through knowledge, desire and emotions.” -Brent Barry.

Structure
- intro
- verse by verse exposition
- relate topic to city life
- what’s the tension, the sin, the idol?
- should always close with “Jesus”, the gospel. “Where is Jesus in this verse?” — Gospel Centered Preaching.
