	- Goal is Thursdays. Fridays the latest. 
1. Here are some guidelines in filing up for the 3 talk-it-over questions — use the Head, Heart, Hand pattern 
	- Head - teaching. Ask a question that talks about the Sunday’s topic, what they learned. 
	- Heart - application. Question that talks about how their lives are affected by what they learned. 
	- Hand - action. This part needs to be about what they need to do immediately after learning and applying what they’ve learned.
