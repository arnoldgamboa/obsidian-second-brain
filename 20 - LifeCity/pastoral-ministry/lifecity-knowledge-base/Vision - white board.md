**(latest version, highlighting gospel-centeredness and strategies)**
**To be a church that brings Life to the city through gospel-centered worship, gospel-centered teachings and gospel-centered communities leading to a gospel-centered living.**

“brings Life to the city"
- brings Life, unapologetically evangelistic
- Life, Jesus
- City, we’re a city-specific church. Experiences of city dwellers are unique and specific. Approach should then be specific, too.

“through"
- these will be our 3 primary ways to bring Life to the City.

“gospel-centered"
- everything that we do will stand on the fact of the gospel, that we are saved by the grace of God, and that nothing we can add on to that, except give glory to Jesus and Jesus alone. The same grace we dispense to bring Life to the city and  live a life to the full"

3 strategies
“gospel-centered worship"
- Sunday Experience
- LifeKids

“gospel-centered teachings"
- all teachings on Sundays, LifeGroups and Outreach events will be filtered through the lens of the gospel.

“gospel-centered communities"
- LifeGroups, our primary personal way of dispensing gospel-centered fellowship in the church

result
“leading to a gospel-centered living"
- these three strategies, by God’s grace when executed correctly, will lead people to live a gospel-centered, Jesus-filled, joyful living in the city.
