Definition of a Disciple:
A disciple is a person who is following Jesus, is being transformed by Jesus, and is committed to the mission of Jesus.

- Highlight “being transformed by Jesus” for today’s message

Bold claim: Life change  happens best in the context of relationships
- Yes, your life changes when you hear a sermon
- …or when you read the Bible
- …or when you pray
- But nothing changes your life — for the better or for the worse — when living life together.
- Give some examples in real life

We are meant to live life together in the church.
- That’s why we see lots of “one another” verses in the Bible

At LifeCity, we organize around LifeGroups. We believe that you’d grow better and faster in your spiritual life in the context of a healthy small group setting.


3 ways that lets you grow in a LifeGroup

1. Because of a life to follow

Paul:
“Follow my example as I follow the example of Christ.” 1 Cor. 1:11
- We need spiritual mentors. And we need “older” people to follow — not just in age but in spiritual maturity.
- We learn through experience. But we avoid known problems through the experience of others.
- “K Prudy”, I joke about his age, but not really about his age. .I need k Prudy as a spiritual guide being a spiritually matured Christian.
- “Pol” - Pol is a blessing. We need more matured Christians in the church were we can get matured guidance
- Look for spiritual mentor in the LifeGroup


1. A life to walk alongside with

- We need Christian friends who will walk along side us, will tell us things as it is not sugar coat it.
- Will provide encouragement, will be shoulder to cry on
- But also, will be honest with us when we need the truth
- Example: Iron men - Clark, Mike, Carlo’s relationship

1. A life to lead

- We need to step up and look for people to disciple. A lot in the church or even outside the church need guidance. 
- We just need to be a step ahead to lead
- All of us are leaders if we have some kind of influence
- Look for someone to mentor in the LifeGroup
- Younger, or less matured Christian, new Christian

These 3 lives are reason why we need to be part of the LifeGroup. 
- We are these 3 lives. We need to look for spiritual guides, we need someone to walk with us, and we need to mentor someone (be a Paul to a Timothy”
