<span style="font-size:18.66666603088379pt;"><b>Leading With Vision / Session Follow-along Guide</b></span>

*“Do what you believe in. Paint a picture of the future. Go there. People will follow.”*
**DOʼs:**
1. Do OWN the vision. In the beginning this is the sole responsibility of the leader.

2. Constantly COMMUNICATE the vision.
When you are sick of saying and hearing it, they are just beginning to get it.

3. Focus more on STORIES than on details. Stories paint the picture. They need a picture.
4. Hold your CREDIBILITY for when it counts. Part of the art of casting vision is knowing when and on what to spend some of the leaderʼs credibility he has worked for.
5. Have an ACED up your sleeve. “There *is a difference in having faith and having the* *ʻgift* *of stupidʼ.”*
– Craig Groeschel
6. Avoid the DENIABILITY trap. When you blow it, own it. On the other hand, when you succeed give all of the credit to God and others.
**DONʼTS:**
1. Donʼt look for CONSENSUS.
*“Your destiny does not lie with those who are willing to walk away.”*
2. Donʼt lose them in the DETAILS. As much as you love the brilliance of your plan and how it will all work... they really only care about how itʼs going to impact lives... theirs and others.
3. Donʼt over PROMISE, over DELIVER. People hate being sold. They love being surprised.
4. Donʼt MANIPULATE. Vision is a gift you are sharing with someone. Using people as a means to an end is SIN.
-- Seth Godin
5. Donʼt LIE. Integrity matters. Transparency and integrity are not the same thing.
6. Donʼt DREAM out loud. Dream three steps ahead,
Plan two steps ahead, Cast vision one step ahead, and Live in the moment.
Stewarding a vision is not dreaming... It requires thinking, planning, setting goals, meeting goals, leadership and.... ACTION.
**GIVING:**
People give to VISION not debt or stuff. People give to generous organizations. 
People give to organizations who have the arrows pointed out. People stay ENGAGED when they see short term wins. People connect with story.... tie giving to stories.
