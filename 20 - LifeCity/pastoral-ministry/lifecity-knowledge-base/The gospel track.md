GOD'S LOVE
"For God so loved the world..
God is a loving God. His love is the
greatest love the world has ever
known. God is love and He loves you!
GOD'S GIFT
"that He gave his only Son..
Man is a sinner. No one is excused.
The result of sin is man has been
separated from God without a right
relationship with Him. But God gave
His Son. Jesus Christ, to be the perfect
sacrifice so man can be restored to a
right relationship with Him.
GOD'S INVITATION
"that whoever believes in Him...
The invitation of God is for all people.
No one is too sinful for His grace. No
sinner is beyond God's forgiveness.
GOD'S PROMISE
..should not perish but have eternal
life."
God is a promise-keeping God. You
can be saved through faith. Faith is
simply believing that He gives eternal
life to those who trust Jesus. Trust Him.
He paid it all!
