<span style="font-size:24pt;"><b>Job Overview</b></span>

**Title** : Care Pastor
**Reports to**: Lead Pastor

<span style="font-size:18pt;"><b>LifeCity Team Culture</b></span>

As staff of LifeCity Church we are committed to being:

- **Family** – We believe that we can only be effective in ministering to our body if we are ministering and caring for one another as brothers and sisters in Christ. We are not first co-workers; we are first the family of God. 
- **Servants** – Our team will adopt the posture of chief servants for our entire church community. Because Christ came in the form of a servant to serve and not to rule, we long to obtain the character of our King and adopt the posture of servant to one another, other teams, and the church at large. 
- **Equippers** – The chief objective and metric of our team is the development of disciples and leaders in the mission of God. The primary task of our team is cultivating disciples and leaders to the glory of God, regardless of the job function performed. 
- **Excellent** – Both the people we serve and the work we do will reflect the magnificence of God and our desire to work with all our heart, and with all the urgency as unto the Lord and not to men. 

These core team identities will dictate how we engage with one another, with the rest of our staff team, and among our wider church body.

<span style="font-size:24pt;"><b>Job Description</b></span>
<span style="font-size:18pt;"><b>Priority of Tasks and Responsibilities</b></span>
**Leadership Development**
- <span style="color:#272727ff;">Coach LifeGroup (LG) leaders through implementation of a sustainable coaching system</span> 
- <span style="color:#272727ff;">Identify, recruit, and train new LG leaders and apprentices</span> 
- <span style="color:#272727ff;">Lead and be the primary equipper at all the monthly LG Trainings</span>  

**LifeGroup DNA**
- <span style="color:#272727ff;">Ensure the health, growth, direction, and reproduction of LifeGroups and LG leaders</span> 
- <span style="color:#272727ff;">Cast vision in various settings to help people become healthy members/disciples</span> 
- <span style="color:#272727ff;">Develop and diagnose health assessment of LifeGroups</span> 
- <span style="color:#272727ff;">Oversee and develop pathways that help people get into LifeGroups</span> 
- <span style="color:#272727ff;">Fight for alignment and movement for discipleship philosophy</span>  

**Content Development**
- <span style="color:#272727ff;">Develop training content, LifeGroup leader content, apprentice and training, etc.</span> 
- <span style="color:#272727ff;">Gather and share stories of how God is at work in LifeGroups</span> 

**Discipleship at large**
- <span style="color:#272727ff;">Oversee strategies and implementation of discipleship strategies</span> 
- <span style="color:#272727ff;">Create, lead, oversee discipleship opportunities through creating new LifeGroups</span> 
- <span style="color:#272727ff;">Oversee discipleship through Marriage/counseling areas</span> 

**Preaching/Teaching**
- <span style="color:#272727ff;">As part of the teaching team, preach on Sundays, and other areas of ministry when called to by the Lead Pastor</span> 

**Other Pastoral Duties**
<span style="color:#272727ff;">As part of the pastoral team, the following duties shall be performed as needed and as requested by the Lead Pastor</span>
- <span style="color:#272727ff;">Wedding ceremonies</span> 
- <span style="color:#272727ff;">Hospital visits</span> 
- <span style="color:#272727ff;">Funeral services</span> 

<span style="font-size:18pt;"><b>What Success Looks Like</b></span>

- <span style="color:#272727ff;">Leaders are walking with God and well equipped for ministry. They are growing in gospel fluency and pastoral ability to disciple others</span> 
- <span style="color:#272727ff;">LifeGroups are healthy—people are experiencing the reality of learning the gospel, living as a family, being on mission together and serving together.</span> 
- <span style="color:#272727ff;">Hitting clear and measurable metrics in evangelism, discipleship, leader development and group reproduction</span>  
- <span style="color:#272727ff;">Leaders are sustaining and learning to disciple people from season to season</span>  
- <span style="color:#272727ff;">Stories of how God is at work in LifeGroups are regularly being shared at various levels</span> 
- <span style="color:#272727ff;">LifeGroups are connected to the larger vision, events, and communication of LifeCity</span> 
- <span style="color:#272727ff;">Special projects and events are executed with excellence that accelerate the mission</span> 
- <span style="color:#272727ff;">Working in friendship, ownership, and humility with all others on staff to help accomplish the mission of LifeCity</span>
