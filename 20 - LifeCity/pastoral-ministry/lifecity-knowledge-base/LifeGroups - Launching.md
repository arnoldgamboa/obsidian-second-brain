New LifeGroup Strategy

**The “Closed” LG System**

- To provide a PREDICTABLE small group environment where participants experience authentic community and spiritual growth

ABC’s of group life
- Accountability
- Belonging
- Care

People care for people they know well

- Groups will sign a covenant to stay together from 1 year to 1 year and a half and challenged to multiply after the period

- Multiplication is mandatory. We don’t want to become an insider focused church

**ESSENTIAL ROLES OF A LIFEGROUP LEADER**

The role of a leader is not necessarily to lead discussion. Everyone should have a chance to do it. Here are the leader’s roles:

**1. Think Life-Change**

Main purpose is to “lead people to become fully devotional followers of Christ”. This is not just a “barkadahan”, or a “share your secret” group. The group should be challenged to be accountable, pursue God’s holiness. That’s why we encourage a predictable environment so people will have authentic community to grow spiritually with.

**2. Cultivate Relationships**

Leaders should build a sense of community in the group.
Activities like:
- Volunteering together in church
- Family get-together
- Prayer time

**3. Promote Participation**

More than sharing the right answers, we want people to share their lives.

**4. Replace Yourself**

Intentionally apprentice someone in their group for future leadership

**5. Provide Care**

People instinctively care for those whom they have a relationship. We should be known to be the “most caring church”. This is accomplished if all members of each group are cared for by their leaders and the group.

**6. Multiply Influence**

Intentionally multiplying their groups opens the door for others to participate in group life.  It multiply their influence. 

**LIFEGROUP RESPONSIBILITES**
- Report Weekly Attendance
- Record change life stories (to be shared to the church, with permission)
- Attend training with LG Supervisor
