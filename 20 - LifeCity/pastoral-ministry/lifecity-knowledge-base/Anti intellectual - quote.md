The anti-intellectual spirit of our times declares: “I don’t need to study. I don’t need to know the Bible. All I need is to have a personal relationship with Jesus.” 
*That viewpoint is on a collision course with what the Wisdom Literature teaches. 
*The purpose for learning the things of God is the acquisition of wisdom, and we cannot have wisdom without knowledge. 
*Ignorance breeds foolishness, but true knowledge — the knowledge of God — leads to the wisdom that is more precious than rubies and pearls.~ R.C. Sproul
