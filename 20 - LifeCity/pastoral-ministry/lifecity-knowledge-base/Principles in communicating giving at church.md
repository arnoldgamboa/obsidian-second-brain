1. It should start from the perspective of worship, not from the one giving
2. Avoid phrases like “10% is the basic”, “tithing is the starting point”, “give more than the tithe”. Although these are true, it gives emphasis on giving (sounds compulsory) and not on the joy of giving (worship)
3. People give to support a vision, not to support a need. 
     - paint a big vision, not a big need
     - encourage people to support the vision, and not support a need

Possible ways to deliver the message:
- what you give helps us reach people for Christ
- a life story. because of you, this story happened
- a story of church generosity. ie. church supporting bag943 or world vision
- a story of individual generosity, ie. a couple giving tithes and God blessed them, if not financially — it can be spiritually or emotionally
- 3-month tithing-challenge 
- support the vision (ie. give because we’re out to reach the city of Makati). Supporting a need (and should not be done) ie. give because we are falling behind our rent.
