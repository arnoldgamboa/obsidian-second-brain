Omnipotent

Omni- all
Potent- power

The attribute of [omnipotence](https://ref.ly/logosref/bk.pt:God$5FPower) refers to God’s unconditioned power to do that which he wills in accordance with his nature.

He speaks things in existence.

Let there be light… and there was light.
Genesis 1: 3

God does what he is pleased to do.

Psalm 115:3 (HCSB)
Our God is in heaven 
and does whatever He pleases. 

Nothing is too hard for him.

Genesis 18:14 (HCSB): 
Is anything impossible for the Lord?  At the appointed time I will come back to you, and in about a year she will have a son.” 


Jesus, the incarnate God, is omnipotent. He can do things that are humanly impossible.
- Calm the storm
- Raised the dead to life (Lazarus)
- He fed 5000 men with 5 loves and 2 fish

	- We want God to fix our problem.
	- God is powerful, can can do, or NOT do things.

		- Get a girlfriend
