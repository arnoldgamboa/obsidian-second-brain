<span style="color:#141823ff;">Don't marry a person for his/her</span>
<span style="color:#141823ff;">- looks; kumukupas at nade-deform.</span>
<span style="color:#141823ff;">- money; puedeng mawala.</span>
<span style="color:#141823ff;">- fame; lumalaos ang sikat.</span>
<span style="color:#141823ff;">- power; puedeng humina ang malakas.</span>
<span style="color:#141823ff;">Don't even marry for just love; emotion lang yan. Madaling maiba / mabago.</span>
<span style="color:#141823ff;">Marry a person for his/her brain ---for the way she/ he thinks! How people think creates their actions Actions create habits. Habits create character. Character shapes day-to-day moments and long seasons of life and lifestyle.</span>
<span style="color:#141823ff;">It is your partner's character --- borne of his/her brain --- that really matters.</span>

<span style="color:#141823ff;">Marry soneone whose thoughts, values, philosophies and aspirations you would love to live with, live on and live for!</span>
