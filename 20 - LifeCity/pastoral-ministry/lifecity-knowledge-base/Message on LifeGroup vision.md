- see how the early church -- bare bones, no lights, sounds, music team, rented hotel function room 

**42They devoted themselves to the apostles’ teaching and to fellowship, to the breaking of bread and to prayer.** 
- focused on learning, understanding the teachings of the apostle 
- which is the teachings of Jesus 
- fresh from their minds, jesus teaching 
- apostles expounding  
- they teach jesus' teachings seriously 
- they want to hear more, know more 

- ***church passionate to know more about Jesus and his teachings*** 

- **fellowship, with believers** 
	- they love being together 

 
- **breaking of bread** 
	- the lords' supper, communion 
	- it could mean that they have fun eating together 
	- they love being together 

**43Everyone was filled with awe at the many wonders and signs performed by the apostles**
- the church is seeing miracles after miracles happening in their midst 
- showing that the power and presence of the holy spirit is with them 
	- those of you who are seasoned christians, you know when the holy spirit's presence is felt 
	- you know when the spirit is working in your life, you are closer to god 
	- one of the way to see this is through the church 

**44All the believers were together and had everything in common. 45They sold property and possessions to give to anyone who had need.** 
- wonderful picture of a loving community 
- they share their resources to one another 
- when someone is in need, they share their blessings 
- they will not let someone in the church suffer 
	- Some say this is early form of socialism and communism 
	- it's not. 
	- people do this from the heart, they are not forced 

**46Every day they continued to meet together in the temple courts. They broke bread in their homes and ate together with glad and sincere hearts, 47praising God and enjoying the favor of all the people.** 
- they value meeting together 
- not about the location 
- not about the food or the sounds or the service 
- they just love being together 
- breaking bread 
- praising God together
