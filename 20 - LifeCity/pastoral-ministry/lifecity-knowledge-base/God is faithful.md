We value faithfulness. Gusto natin, faithful ang asawa, ang boyfriend, girlfriend, di ba? Ang kaso, our experience is different. We experience betrayal from people we love. Unfaithfulness is all over our relationships. Unfortunately, this experience is translated into how we see God. Can God really be trusted? Is he even faithful to his promises?

The Bible says, “The Lord your God is indeed God. He is the faithful God who keeps his covenant for a thousand generations and lavishes his unfailing love on those who love him and obey his commands.” That means it is ingrained in his nature to be faithful. He never knew how to be unfaithful. When he promises something, itaga mo sa bato, he will never disappoint you. He will be able to deliver.

Maybe the reason why we don’t appreciate God’s faithfulness is, well, because we don’t really know what promises. According to scholars, there are 8,810 promises of God in the Bible. Kung alam mo lang ang mga promises ng Diyos sayo, you’ll enjoy and love God’s character. Let me give you  what I consider 3 of the most special promise of God:

**God promises to be with you in tough times.**  He never promised that we will not experience troubles. What he promises is that he will walk with us through tough times. He’ll never leave us nor forsake us.

**God promises to let nothing separate you from him.**  Not death, not life, not the present or future, not anything in this world can ever separate us from his love. That’s his promise. Whatever happens, whether we’re faithful or not, he loves us unconditionally.

**God promises to come back and bring us home with him.** This world really is not our home. He promised that there will be time, and it’s coming, that Jesus will come back and bring us home with him. No more wars, no move sickness, no more covid, no more evil things. All joy and peace and worship of God. 

So, if ever you feel doubting God’s promises, isipin mo nalang — itaga mo sa bato, God is faithful.
