Here are the things that need to be checked by the Experience Coordinator. Should all be done 30 mins before the Experience starts:

1. Stage 
	- 2 stages set up 
	- No wires on stage 
1. Audio/Lights 
	- sound checked properly 
	- <b><i><u>both speakers (left/right) are working properly</u></i></b> 
	- if possible, monitor working 
	- **Lights in right position pointing to the speaker, not over illuminating the screen** 
	- Light cables not interfering the isle and seats 
1. Seats 
	- At least 60 seats in place with about 10 in stack 
	- **Seats properly aligned to new stage set up** 
	- Seats have the bibles hang 
1. Outside the hall 
	- Signages placed correctly 
		- at the door 
		- at the elevator 
	- Program guide set by the welcome team 
	- Coffee table working properly. If not, notify hotel staff 
1. LifeKids 
	- Room set up properly 
	- Coordinate with LifeKids volunteer what else is needed 
1. **Ensures that sound check ends 30 mins before and that the teams start to gather for huddle time.**
