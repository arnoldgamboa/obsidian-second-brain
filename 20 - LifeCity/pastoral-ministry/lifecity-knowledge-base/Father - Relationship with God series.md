Matt 6: 9-13

A relationship with the Father involves daily obedience and childlike trust.

As a father, He: 
1. Deserves honor and respect - “hallowed by your name"
2. Demands obedience to his will
3. Provides
4. Forgives our sins
5. Protects 

Why are we unable to have a close relationship with the Father?
- we had wrong perspective through our earthly fathers
- we are consumed by religion
- we are in sin

How do we relate with the Father?
1. With Reverence 
2. With Dependence
3. With Thankfulness
4. With Trust
