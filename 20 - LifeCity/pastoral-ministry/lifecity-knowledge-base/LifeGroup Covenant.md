<p style="text-align:center;margin:0"><span style="font-family:Georgia-Bold;font-size:15pt;color:#333333ff;"><b>LIFEGROUP COVENANT</b></span>
</p>
<span style="font-family:Georgia-Bold;font-size:15pt;color:#333333ff;"><b>Purpose</b></span>
<span style="font-family:Georgia;font-size:15pt;color:#333333ff;">To provide a predictable environment where participants experience authentic community and spiritual growth.</span>
<span style="font-family:Georgia-Bold;font-size:15pt;color:#333333ff;"><b>Values & Goals</b></span>
<span style="font-family:Georgia-Italic;font-size:15pt;color:#333333ff;"><i>Relationships</i></span>  <span style="font-family:Georgia;font-size:15pt;color:#333333ff;">While prayer and discussion of curriculum are key elements of a community group, the driving force behind the group is the building of relationships.</span>
<span style="font-family:Georgia-Italic;font-size:15pt;color:#333333ff;"><i>Authenticity</i></span>  <span style="font-family:Georgia;font-size:15pt;color:#333333ff;">The atmosphere of a community group should encourage openness and transparency among members. This is an environment where people should feel free to be themselves.</span>
<span style="font-family:Georgia-Italic;font-size:15pt;color:#333333ff;"><i>Confidentiality</i></span>  <span style="font-family:Georgia;font-size:15pt;color:#333333ff;">For authenticity to occur, members must be able to trust that issues discussed within a community group will not be shared outside the group.</span>
<span style="font-family:Georgia-Italic;font-size:15pt;color:#333333ff;"><i>Respect</i></span> <span style="font-family:Georgia;font-size:15pt;color:#333333ff;">Group members should never say anything that will embarrass their spouse or members of the group.</span>
<span style="font-family:Georgia-Italic;font-size:15pt;color:#333333ff;"><i>Availability</i></span>  <span style="font-family:Georgia;font-size:15pt;color:#333333ff;">A primary responsibility of community is to prioritize for specific relationships. This requires a willingness to be available to meet each other’s needs.</span>
<span style="font-family:Georgia-Italic;font-size:15pt;color:#333333ff;"><i>Multiplication</i></span>  <span style="font-family:Georgia;font-size:15pt;color:#333333ff;">Group members recognize that one of the goals of their group is to start a new group within twelve to eighteen months. This allows others to experience the community group relationship.</span>
<span style="font-family:Georgia-Bold;font-size:15pt;color:#333333ff;"><b>Group Guidelines</b></span>
<span style="font-family:Georgia;font-size:15pt;color:#333333ff;">1. The group will meet from________through________.</span>
<span style="font-family:Georgia;font-size:15pt;color:#333333ff;">2. The group will meet on________afternoon/night.</span>
<span style="font-family:Georgia;font-size:15pt;color:#333333ff;">3. The group sessions will begin at________and end at________.</span>
<span style="font-family:Georgia;font-size:15pt;color:#333333ff;">4. The group time will typically consist of________ minutes of sharing,________of study/discussion, and________of prayer.</span>
<span style="font-family:Georgia;font-size:15pt;color:#333333ff;">5. The group will be a closed group until all members agree to additional members.</span>
<span style="font-family:Georgia;font-size:15pt;color:#333333ff;">6. Group members will attend and participate on a regular basis. Members agree to pray for other group members on a weekly basis.</span>
<span style="font-family:Georgia;font-size:15pt;color:#333333ff;">I covenant together with the other members of this group to honor this agreement.</span>
