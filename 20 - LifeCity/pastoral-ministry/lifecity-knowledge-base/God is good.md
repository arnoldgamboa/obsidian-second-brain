# Is God really good all the time?

If you’ve been to a Christian church before, you’ve probably heard people say “God is good, all the time, and all the time God is good!” 

Do you really believe that when you declare it? That God is good, all the time? 

How about when there’s 288,000 people died in the Pacific rim caused by a tsunami in 2004 — is God still good? Or when your friends or loved ones died of covid19 just recently? Or when a child is raped or an innocent man is gunned down, or when you find out you have cancer, or when you lost your job, or when your husband left you — is God still good?

Madali lang sabihin na God is good di ba? Pero may basis ba tayo pag sinasabi natin yan? Tanong nga ng mga kaibigan nating atheists, “If there’s really a good God, why does he allow the presence of evil?”

Here’s how I’d explain the presence of suffering.

First and foremost, if there’s a good God, which I believe there is — one of the good things he did is to allow free will to exist. That’s what he gave our forefathers Adam and Eve. Free will is an essential component for us human beings to have an authentic relationship with our creator. Without free will, we’re just robots. But God doesn’t want that, he want to have real, engaging relationship with us.

Yun nga lang, the presence of free will allows the possibility of sin. That’s exactly what happened in the garden of Eden when Adam and Even chose to rebel against God. Sin entered the world, which caused our world to deteriorate — kaya may earthquakes, tsunamis, typhoons and pandemics. We are all victims of a deteriorating world.

Sin is why we hurt one another and even hurt ourselves. That’s why we experience betrayal, pains, sickness, suffering. 

But God is still good because he solved the problem of sin  when he sent Jesus to fix what’s broken. He sacrificed himself on the cross to redeem the world. That’s the good news. That’s the gospel. We can’t fix our sin problem and suffering, but God made a way. He fixed it through Jesus Christ.

God promised that he will end suffering once and for all in the future, but in the meantime, he said we’re still going suffer, but take heart because Jesus has conquered the world. 

Kahit may paghihirap, kasa-kasama natin ang Dios. Kaya may kapayapaan tayong nararanasan sa puso natin. There’s security, there’s joy, there’s peace in the midst of suffering.

So, when we say God is Good all the time, we just don’t say it without proof. Jesus is the proof that God is good. Kapit lang, kasama natin ang Dios.
