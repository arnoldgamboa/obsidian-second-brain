***THE APOSTLE’S CREED***



- one of the earliest creeds created by the church

**WHAT’S A CREED FOR?**
- for spiritual formation
- to protect the flock

**WHY RECITE THE APOSTLE’S CREED?**
- still being done by creedal churches (Lutherans, Presbyterians, some Baptists, Roman Catholics)
- pledging allegiance to the God of the Bible

(Invite congregation to stand)

*"I believe in God the Father almighty, creator of heaven and earth, and in Jesus Christ, his only Son, our Lord, who was conceived by the Holy Spirit, born of the Virgin Mary, suffered under Pontius Pilate, was crucified, dead, and buried.* 




*He descended into hell and the third day he rose again from the dead. He ascended into heaven and sits on the right hand of the Father almighty, from whence he shall come to judge the living and the dead.* 

*I believe in the Holy Spirit, the holy catholic church, the communion of the saints, the forgiveness of sins, the resurrection of the body, and life everlasting, Amen.”*


**HE DESCENDED TO HELL, THE THIRD DAY HE ROSE AGAIN FROM THE DEAD**

Matt. 27:32-28:10


- let’s not turn away our eyes on the brutality of the cross
- seriousness of God against sin

**WHY ‘DESCENDED TO HELL’?**

- A lot leave out “descended to hell” because he truly did not descend to physical hell
- But it should be there

- He bears the weight of our sin
- “Jesus took on all our transgressions”
- Became the curse for us, so we might be a righteousness of God

 “My God, My God why have you forsaken me”
- the only time Jesus called Him, “My God”, not Father.
- There was separation between the Son and the Father for the first time in ALL ETERNITY
- (You can share an example of how you were once separated from your dad for a while. How does that feel?) 
- HELL is the absence of God to bless and the presence of God to judge

- Didn’t mean that Jesus went to a physical hell
- He ‘experienced’ hell — the absence of God to bless and the presence of God to judge
- It is our sin who held him there. It’s our hell, he descends into OUR hell

APPLICATION PART OF THE MESSAGE

**Establishes our faith**
- If  Christ died on the cross, all your sins, past present future are forgiven.
- Be established on that! It’s been paid for. Your don’t owe anything. 

**Creates balance**
- Christ’s death and resurrection means your sins are paid
- BUT, repentance means you acknowledge that. It does not do any good for you, even how great this truth is, without repenting, without your life turning around from being you as the King of your life to Jesus being the King of your life.

**Changes our lives**
- “Because he lives, I can face tomorrow. Because he lives, all fear is gone. Because I know, He holds the future. And life is worth the living, just because He lives”
