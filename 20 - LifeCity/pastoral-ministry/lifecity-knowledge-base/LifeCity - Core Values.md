<span style="color:#222222ff;">THE CODE</span>

<span style="color:#222222ff;">WE WILL DO ANYTHING SHORT OF SIN TO REACH PEOPLE WHO DON'T KNOW CHRIST.</span>
<span style="color:#222222ff;">To reach people no one is reaching, we have to do things no one is doing.</span>

<span style="color:#222222ff;">WE TRULLY BELIEVE THAT FOUND PEOPLE FIND PEOPLE.</span>
We will passionately create opportunities for people to encounter Jesus in a profound way.

<span style="color:#222222ff;">WE WILL ALWAYS REMEMBER THAT EVERYONE IS IN A UNIQUE SPOT IN THEIR SPIRITUAL JOURNEY.</span>
<span style="color:#222222ff;">It doesn't matter who they are, where they've been or what they've done, they have a place here.</span>

<span style="color:#222222ff;">WE ARE FAITH-FILLED, BIG THINKING, ALL OR NOTHING RISK TAKERS.</span>
<span style="color:#222222ff;">We'll never insult God with small thinking and safe living.</span>

<span style="color:#222222ff;">WE WILL HOLD THE BIBLE CLOSE TO OUR HEART DAILY.</span>
<span style="color:#222222ff;">It nourishes our soul and renews our mind.</span>

<span style="color:#222222ff;">WE'RE ALL ABOUT THE 'CAPITAL C' CHURCH.</span>
<span style="color:#222222ff;">The local church is the hope of the world and we know we can accomplish more together than apart.</span>

<span style="color:#222222ff;">WE ALWAYS BRING OUR BEST.</span>
<span style="color:#222222ff;">Excellence honors God and inspires people.</span>

<span style="color:#222222ff;">WE ARE SPIRITUAL CONTRIBUTORS NOT SPIRITUAL CONSUMERS.</span>
<span style="color:#222222ff;">The church does not exist for us. We are the church and we exist for the world.</span>

<span style="color:#222222ff;">WE WILL LEAD THE WAY WITH IRRATIONAL GENEROSITY.</span>
<span style="color:#222222ff;">We truly believe it is more blessed to give than to receive.</span>

<span style="color:#222222ff;">WE WILL LAUGH HARD, LOUD AND OFTEN.</span> 
<span style="color:#222222ff;">Nothing is more fun than serving God with people you love.</span>
