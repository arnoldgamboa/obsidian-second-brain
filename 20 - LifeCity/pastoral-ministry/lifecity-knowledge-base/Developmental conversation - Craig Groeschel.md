How to start a coversation

1. Things are not going well
- tell the truth

2. I want you to succeed and I’ll make everything I can to help you succeed

3. For you to stay, here’s what needs to happen in the next 2 weeks ( or x no. of days)

4. We’re going to meet regularly to check your progress. (how are things going, what are you learning, how are you improving)

5. If you can’t get there, then we’re going to make a change

If you as the leader don’t <b><u>help them to grow or empower them to go</u></b>, the problem is you. You’re holding the organization back.
