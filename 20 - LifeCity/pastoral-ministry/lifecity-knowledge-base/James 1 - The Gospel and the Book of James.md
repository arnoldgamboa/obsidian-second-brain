James: Gospel for Everyday Life.

Why do we need the gospel everyday?

Everyday we’re tempted by pride.

Everyday we’re tempted to despair.

Everyday we’re tempted to sin.

Everyday we’re tempted to fear

“Preach the gospel to yourself.”

James:
In chapter 1, we’ll see trials and Christian perseverance that will move into hearing and doing the Word of God. In chapter 2, we look at the sin of partiality and the relationship between works and deeds. 
In chapter 3, we see the taming of the tongue and wisdom coming from God and not from our hearts. In chapter 4, you get a warning against worldliness and boasting in tomorrow, being arrogant. In chapter 5, we see a warning against trusting in riches and comfort as a satisfier of the longings of our hearts. We see a call to patient suffering and prayer.  


<span style="font-size:24pt;color:#a90eff;"><b>humility, confidence, holiness and hope</b></span>


Chapter 1 - Trials, perseverance. Hearing, doing the Word of God.
Chapter 2 - Sin of partiality. Faith and works. 
Chapter 3 - Our speech, wisdom from God
Chapter 4 - Worldliness. Arrogance.
Chapter 5 - Riches of this world. Patience in suffering.
