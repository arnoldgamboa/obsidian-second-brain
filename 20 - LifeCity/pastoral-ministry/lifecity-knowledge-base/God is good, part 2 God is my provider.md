**What-Know - KEYTHOUGHT:** Believe that God provides for our future therefore, do not worry.
**Why-Know**: Worry kills our joy and trust in God who provides everything we need.
**What-Do:** Kill worry, trust God.
**Why-Do:** Fully trusting God for our future and provision provides peace.
