Without honest and timely feedback, your personal and organizational growth is always limited. 

Everyone is blind so much about themselves.

We don’t know what we don’t know.

- Feedback is the number one tool to personal and organizational growth 
- Feedback is common, but helpful feedback is rare. 
- Decide: We will develop a culture of honest, timely and helpful feedback. 

Areas:
- teams 
- discipleship 


Factors in receiving feedback

1. Don’t dread feedback; crave it. 
2. Separate the do from the who 
	1. we feel like it’s a critique of who we are 
	2. “this is just the way i am”. 
	3. should be “i’m not fixed in the way I do" 
	4. when you are getting defensive, the more you need to listen 
1. Ask clarifying questions 
	1. General questions rarely lead to specific feedback 
	2. Be specific 
	3. Don’t say “that’s not true”, say, “tell me more, help me understand"
