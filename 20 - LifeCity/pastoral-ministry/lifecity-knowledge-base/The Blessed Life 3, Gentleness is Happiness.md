“Meek”
praus (gr)
Used to describe a tamed stalion.

Common wrong understanding of what meekness/gentleness is:

Weekness/timidity
- lack strength or courage
- lack of assertiveness or the inability to stand up for oneself. 
- shy, easily dominated or influenced by others
Strength under control
- Controlled emotions
- Courage to respond in grace and humility under pressure

Passivity
- Doesn’t take any action
- Waits for something to happen
Active restraint 
- Thoughtful and purposeful action

Submissiveness
- Yield to others without resistance 
- Reluctance to express opposition 
Humble confidence
- Yield to the will of God
- Prioritize others without losing identity

Meekness is not weakness; gentleness is strength under control.
