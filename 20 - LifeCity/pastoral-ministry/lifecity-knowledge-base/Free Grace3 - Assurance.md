**Know that the born again experience (justification) and Christian growth (sanctification) are two separate seasons of your Christian walk.**

<span style="color:#ff436ff;"><b>Justification</b></span> **| Sanctification**

- <span style="color:#432ffff;"><i>Born again experience</i></span>
- You’re new
- Something happens inside of you, your spirit.
- Some people repents of known sin, others change of perspective

<span style="color:#0313ff;"><b>Justification</b></span> **|** <span style="color:#ff436ff;"><b>Sanctification</b></span>

*Sanctification is a day to day experience.*
- Ups and downs
- Sometimes you are making strides, sometimes you’re failing…
- Because of this…

— you need to be able to separate these two seasons of your life.
- Do not interchange
- When you do, you’ll doubt your salvation 
- Salvation is by grace alone through faith in Jesus alone not based on your works

**Know that your assurance is based on a firm foundation — God’s promise.**

'I am sure of this, that he who started a good work in you will carry it on to completion until the day of Christ Jesus. '
Philippians 1:6 (CSB)

“I am sure of this” - Paul is sure
“He” — who? God the Father
“Started a good work in you” - from zero, you’re making progress, god started it

“Until the day of Christ Jesus” - Sanctification process is on going.

Foundation of your assurance is God’s promise.
