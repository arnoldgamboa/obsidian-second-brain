**Truths about God**

1. God is ONE
	- Deut. 6:4 - Hear, O Israel: The LORD our God, the LORD is one.
	- Isaiah 46:9 - "remember the former things of old; for I am God, and there is no other; I am God, and there is none like me"
1. The Father is God
	- Romans 1:7 - "To all those in Rome who are loved by God and called to be saints: Grace to you and peace from God our Father and the Lord Jesus Christ."
1. Jesus is God
	- John 20:28 - Thomas answered him, “My Lord and my God!”
	- John 1:1 - "In the beginning was the Word, and the Word was with God, and the Word was God."
1. The Holy Spirit is God
	- Acts 5:3-4 - But Peter said, “Ananias, why has Satan filled your heart to lie to the Holy Spirit and to keep back for yourself part of the proceeds of the land? ... You have not lied to man but to God.”
1. God exist as a Trinity
	- The Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit are distinct persons
		- They have separate emotions
		- All 3 are present during Jesus’ baptism
	- The Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit are co-equal, co-existent persons
	- The doctrine of the Trinity is not found in one verse of the Bible. It is found in studying the whole Bible.
	- TRINITY - God is three in one — Father, Son, and Holy Spirit. He is not three Gods, nor is he one God acting in three different ways. The Bible tells us that God is three different and distinct persons, and that these three different and distinct persons are one in the being of God.
1. CONCLUSION: THE DOCTRINE OF THE TRINITY IS FOUND IN THE BIBLE
	2. All the elements of the doctrine are taught in Scripture
		1. One God
		2. The Father is God
		3. The Son is God
		4. The Holy Spirit is God
		5. The Father, Son, and Holy Spirit are three persons 
	1. The Bible does not forbid using extrabiblical language to define and describe biblical truth. The Bible teaches that "the faith" (which would include the nature of the object of faith, namely God) was "delivered once for all to the saints" (Jude 3).  Therefore any religious movement that claims to have restored the true doctrine of God through any kind of revelation is unbiblical.

**Trinitarian heresies**
1. Tritheism - a believe that  God The Father, God The Son and God the Holy Spirit as 3 separate Gods.
	- Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints (Mormons)
1. Arianism - a belief that  God the Father created Jesus as a God, therefore, the Father and the Son did not always co-exist.
		- Arius of Alexandira (<span style="color:#252525ff;">AD 250–336)</span>
		- Declared heretic by the Council of Nicea in AD 335.
	- Jehova’s Witnesses
1. Semi-Arianism - a belief that God the Father is the Almighty God and Jesus is just a man. The Holy Spirit is the presence of God.
	- Iglesia ni Cristo
1. Modalism - A belief that God the Father, Son and Holy Spirit are not 3 separate persons but 3 manifestations of God.
	- Oneness Pentecostals

**Why is the Correct Understanding of the Trinity so Important?**

1. **Theologically**: Understanding the truth of the Trinity prevents us from adopting inadequate views of God.
1. **Personally** : The Trinity is a reminder of the majesty and mystery of the God who gave himself for us on the cross.
2. **Relationally**: The Trinity show us that God in his very essence is relational.
