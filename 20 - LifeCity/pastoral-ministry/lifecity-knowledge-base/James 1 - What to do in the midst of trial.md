What to do in the midst of trial - 
Find joy.
- Not easy.
- Not impossible. 
- Joy, my wife, encouragement.
- Find joy through your communion with God everyday
- Find joy by getting encouragements from fellow believers
- Find joy by remembering how God has been so good in the past.
- Find joy by knowing this trial matures you, makes you a better person.
