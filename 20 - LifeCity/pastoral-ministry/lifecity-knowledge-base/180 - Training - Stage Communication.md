**LifeChurch Sunday Experience — for Stage Communicators — SEQUENCE:**

1. 180 - Welcome/Announcements (180 seconds)
	- Welcome guests
	- Give your ONE announcement (what do you want them to know, feel, do)
	- Introduce the message/speaker
1. Responses
	- Let people know what to do with their response (check the box on communication card)
1. Tithes and Offerings 
	- Don’t focus on guilt
	- Focus on:
		- blessing through giving
		- church vision
1. Closing/Dismiss
	- Ask people to invite their friends next week
	- Whoever finds God, finds life!
