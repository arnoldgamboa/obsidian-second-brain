Why does God answer “No” to our prayers?

1. When God has a bigger perspective 
	1. God can see the future 
	2. Can see every situation and implications 
	3. “Do you give your children everything they ask for?” No. 
		1. to protect you, from something you can’t see. 
	1. Your perspective is the here and now, God’s perspective is accomplishing his eternal plan 


1. When he has a better plan 
	1. I’m going to say yes, but in a different way 
	2. “My ways are better than your ways" 
	3. My debts — can you approve my loan, so i can pay the other loans? 
 	4. delay to grow our character 

1. When he as a greater purpose 
	1. God will not let your prayer interfere with His purpose 
	2. We pray for God to bail us out of a painful situation.  
		1. god says NO, I’ll let you go through it. through this you’ll grow  

 

One Point: What do you do when God, says “no” Trust His heart.
