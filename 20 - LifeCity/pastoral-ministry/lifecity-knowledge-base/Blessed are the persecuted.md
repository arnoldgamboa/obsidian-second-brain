Blessed are the poor in spirit…

- they know they are spiritually poor.

Blessed are those who mourn…
God grieves with you. The Holy Spirit comforts you. The body of Christ embraces you.

Blessed are the gentle…
Gentleness is the strength that calms chaos with understanding and kindness.

Blessed are those who hunger and thirst for righteousness…
Trusting in Jesus is what makes us righteous in the eyes of God.

Blessed are the merciful…
The best way to experience God’s mercy is to extend it to someone else.

Blessed are the pure in heart…
God is more interested in you  than what other people think about you.

Blessed are the peacemakers…
You are most like God when you’re an agent of reconciliation.
