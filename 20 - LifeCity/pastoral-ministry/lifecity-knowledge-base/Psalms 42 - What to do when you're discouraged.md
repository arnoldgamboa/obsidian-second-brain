**Do you feel discouraged?**

- everyone we encounter are experiencing some kind of struggle inside of them.  
- They can be people around you. Or, they can be you. 
- ME, as I was writing this message, i send a full load of discouragement, what’s going around the future of the church, my livelihood, uncertainty 
- Psalmist can relate 

Ps. 42:5
Why, my soul, are you downcast? Why so disturbed within me? 

NLT
Why am I discouraged?
Why is my heart so sad?

- Feeling down 
- Soak yourself to sadness 
- it can be doubts, fear, financial, relational 
- most are feeling discouraged.  
- business failure, a dream you failed to achieve, a plan that’s unable to execute,  
- a relationship that’s going sideways 
- MAGNIFIED during this time of crisis 
- SOMETIMES, when you experience success — you also experience discouragement! Weird? We’ll find out in a little while. 

“Why so downcast oh my soul?”
- notice he talks to his soul 
- body - Paul said, a “tent" 
- soul - “soul/spirit”, the real you, will live for eternity. 
- soul is the one discouraged 
- talks to his soul 

Sometimes, it’s good to talk to yourself.
- self evaluation 
- self realization 
- self-awareness 

2 ways to encourage yourself — the right way and the wrong way.

The wrong way: toxic positivity
- it’s ok to be positive. Have positive outlook in life. 
- WRONG — **lie to yourself** 
- “You got this, Arnold”, Everything will be fine (Question: how do you know?) 
- “You’re a good person, Arnold. Everything will turn out right” - that’s a lie 
- “Good things happen to good people?” that’s called ‘prosperity gospel. that’s a lie. 

The right way: encourage yourself with the truths about God.
“preaching to yourself"

What do you do when you’re discouraged?

1. Know that what you ultimately long for is God. 

v1,2
As the deer pants for streams of water, so my soul pants for you, my God. My soul thirsts for God, for the living God. When can I go and meet with God?
- bad things happening may be the reason you’re discouraged 
- even when you’re succeeding you experience discouragement 
- eventually, we have to realize that our soul’s ultimate need is God 
- “pants” - uhaw na uhaw 
- Kambingan - suga ng kambing, looking for water 
- “Soul, You know what? You’re discouraged because you are thirsting for God. Crave for God and his presence." 
- Search him, long for him when you’re discouraged. 

1. Remember God’s goodness in the past. 

v5 
My soul is downcast within me; therefore I will remember you from the land of the Jordan, the heights of Hermon—from Mount Mizar.
- when you’re discouraged, remember God’s goodness in the past 
- how he delivered you, how he supplied for your needs 
- your present reality needs to be reminded of the truth in the past 
- God has been faithful 

"Mount Mizar”
- “littleness" 
- feeling little, little how we accomplish, how little people loves us 
- we don’t humble ourselves, we belittle ourselves 
- don’t wallow on your pain  
- instead “worship God” in the midst of your discouragement 
- “I remember you from Mount Mizar" 

1. Put your hope in God 

- sounds simple and cliche, but it’s profound 

v5 
Why, my soul, are you downcast? Why so disturbed within me? Put your hope in God, for I will yet praise him, my Savior and my God.

- if remembering God’s goodness is in the past, putting your hope in God is in the future 
- we put our hope in our careers, in our ability to create wealth, in our relationships. 
- what this crisis magnified is that we really can’t put our hope in these things 
- we’re discraouged when think how our careers may suffer because of this, our relationships 
- those are realities, and it’s ok to think about those,  
- put your hope only in God 

- putting “hope in God” also mean putting your “trust in God" 
- like “putting my weight” in this chair. 
- I “trust this chair that it can hold me, that I will not fall down" 
- that’s like putting your “hope in God” means. 
- You trust that God in control, that he holds the future. That he can carry you through 

What do you do when you’re discouraged? Preach to yourself!

- tell yourself of the truths about God 
- tell yourself of the truths about who you are according to God 
- Tell yourself: 
- God is all I need, I long for him 
- God has been faithful in the past, there’s no reason he won’t be in the future 
- God is in the past, at present and in the future. He knows what’s the future holds 
- God saved me and my future with him for eternity is secured 
- God has forgiven me of all of my sins, past present and future 
- God cares for me and loves me. And even if I may not get what I want, he will give me what I need. 
- In Jesus, I am more than a conqueror.  
- He began his good work in me, he is faithful to complete it. 

- So if you feel discouraged today, preach to your soul 
	- “Why are you down cast o my soul? Why are you so disturbed within me?" 
	- Put your hope in God. 
	- SOUL — PUT YOUR HOPE IN GOD! 

Lord, there might be some bought of doubts that creates a discouraged heart within us. We want to preach your truths to our souls.
