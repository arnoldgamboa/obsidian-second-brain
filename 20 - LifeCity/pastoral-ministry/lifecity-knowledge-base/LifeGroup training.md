Mission: to lead people into a growing relationship with Jesus.

Our primary mode of dispensing this mission is our LIfeGroups.
Discipleship is leading people to make their next step in their relationship with Jesus.

Your role as a LifeGroup Leader

1. You’re a “deacon” <a href="https://www.crosswalk.com/church/pastors-or-leadership/the-role-of-the-deacon-1235697.html" rel="noopener" class="external-link" target="_blank" style="color:#e4afaff;"><u>https://www.crosswalk.com/church/pastors-or-leadership/the-role-of-the-deacon-1235697.html</u></a> 
2. You’re a spiritual guide 
3. You’re a spiritual mentor 
4. You’re the big brother/sister 

6 commitments to a healthy LifeGroup
1. Gospel (grace-centered, jesus-focused message and living) 
2. Leadership (serve to lead) 
3. Warmth (Welcoming, belonging, authentic, hospitable, caring) 
4. Empathy (counsel, guide) 
5. Priority (support) 
6. Neighbour (Evangelism)
