Why we stagnate in our spiritual growth?

1. Lack of healthy spiritual environment  
2. Inability to learn from the Word and develop spiritual disciplines 
3. Darling sins 

God tells you who you are before he tells you what to do.

Because Jesus died for your sin, you can put your sin to death.

- God is Holy 
- God loves us, but he hates sin 
- Our sins activates the wrath of God 
- Jesus is the propitiation for our sins (satisfaction) 

The goal of a Christian is not to be sinless, but to sin less. 

Since Jesus walks with you, you can walk with Jesus.
- you can’t meet Jesus and not change 
- spend jesus in the place of sin 
- fan or follower? 
- fan — yehey jesus! follower — where to, jesus? 
- Follower — whatever Jesus says, you follow 


Christian lip service is easy, Christian lifestyle is hard

We cannot worship God and hate our brother at the same time. 

Its ok not to like our brother or sister. We’re commanded to love, not to like. 

Which area of your relationship with Jesus you think you’ve grown. Which area stagnated? Which are are you neglecting?

Christian life is not living a life like jesus. It is living a life abiding in Jesus.
