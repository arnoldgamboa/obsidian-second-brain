Jesus experienced the full range of human emotions.
- Jesus was joyful (He “Jumped for joy”)

When you’re _____________________, remember — 
“Jesus is human, too.”

<span style="font-family:ArialMT;font-size:16pt;color:#01320ff;">For we do not have a high priest who is unable to sympathize with our weaknesses, but one who in every respect has been tempted as we are, yet without sin. (Heb 4:15)</span>

In his humanity, Jesus wept with his friends. In his divinity, he raised up Lazarus from the dead.

In his humanity, he can sympathize with what I’m going through. In his divinity, he does what only he can do.

Because Jesus is human, too, you can run to him. 
Because Jesus is God, you can run to him and experience peace.

Jesus is human, too.
