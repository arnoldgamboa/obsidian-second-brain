# LifeCity Values - updated

CULTURE is the invisible quality every organization have.

The primary driving force of your culture is your values.

Show me a growing organization and I’ll show you a group of leaders who protect their culture and live out their values.
Show me a declining organization and I’ll show you a group of leaders who abandon their culture and doesn’t live out their values.

## **THE CODE**

**WE TRULY BELIEVE THAT FOUND PEOPLE FIND PEOPLE.**
We will passionately create opportunities for people to encounter Jesus in a profound way.

- We are unapologetically evangelistic
- Insider focused vs Outsider focused
- Healthy church evangelizes (evaluate ourselves if evangelism truly is something that we do)
- We grow wider through evangelism, we grow deeper through discipleship

**WE WILL RECOGNIZE THAT EVERYONE IS IN A UNIQUE SPOT IN THEIR SPIRITUAL JOURNEY.**
It doesn't matter who they are, where they've been or what they've done, they have a place here.

- the value of discipleship
- We are all in our own pace in our growth. We need to value how God formed us
- Mentorship 
- Everything is spiritual 
- Our life is about Jesus. We should grow on all areas of our life. Church should be a venue foe that 
- Church should be a safe place for everyone to be planted and grow.

**WE WILL WALK THROUGH LIFE TOGETHER IN LIFEGROUPS.**
Life is messy, we don't live it alone. We live in loving communities of believers.

- basic church unit is the LifeGroup 
- We can function without big church gathering but we can’t function without a LifeGroup 
- LifeGroup is where real relationship and growth happens
- LifeGroup is also a function of discipleship 
- Mentorship 

**WE WILL HOLD THE BIBLE CLOSE TO OUR HEART DAILY.**
It nourishes our soul and renews our mind.

- the value of daily quiet time
- The Bible shouldn’t just be a “religious” book that we need to read at church, or debate about. It is a book about God and how he relates with us. 
- We should train ourselves to form this habit of consuming the most important content we should consume within the day  the Bible. 

**WE ARE SPIRITUAL CONTRIBUTORS NOT SPIRITUAL CONSUMERS.**
The church does not exist for us. We are the church and we exist for the world.

- the value of using everyone’s spiritual gifts
- We need to move away from consumer mentality. We need to contribute 

**WE WILL LAUGH HARD, LOUD AND OFTEN.** 
Nothing is more fun than serving God with people you love.
