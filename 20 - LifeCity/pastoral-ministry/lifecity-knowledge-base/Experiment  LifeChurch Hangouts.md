**Objective**: To become a bridge between people coming to church, Lifers' friends who are not going to church AND LifeGroup. Practically, this is a step to LifeGroup.

**Win**: Lifers hanging out with people who currently do not  attend LifeGroups.

**General program description:** 

1. The idea of Hangouts is to bridge the gap between the big gathering (Experiences) and the small gathering (LifeGroup) as well as to bridge the gap between pre-Experience attenders and LifeGroups.

2. This is a venue where active Experience and LifeGroup attenders can bring their friends that are not yet attending a LifeGroup. 

3. These friends can be attending Experience already but are not yet attending a LifeGroup, or…

4. Can be friends whom Lifers are having a hard time inviting to Experience and/or LifeGroup

5. Hangouts can be any group activity that involves fellowship, fun and getting-to-know-each-other. Activities may include among others but not limited to: movies, Bancheto "food trips", bowling, basketball games, etc.

**Guidelines:**

1. Anyone can host/arrange a LifeChurch Hangouts meet up. They will need to coordinate with the LifeGroup coordinator (me, for now) as to the time, day and type of activity he is planning to host.

2. These will be announced during Sunday Experience and online. 

3. Hangouts will happen during LifeGroup season breaks (April, August and December)

4. Hangouts can be done anytime and any day of the week.

5. Anyone within the church can join with only one requirement: they should bring along with them at least 1 friend who is not attending a LifeGroup.

6. Ideally, whatever cost that a guest will incur during Hangout (food, movie tickets, etc) will be shouldered by the sponsoring Lifer.

7. All guests' name and contact number will be submitted to the hosts for proper recording and follow up.

8. Eventually, the Lifer will need to be transparent and tell them that this is a LifeChurch sponsored activity.

9. The goal is to follow up and invite them for the following month's LifeGroup season.

10. As the new LifeGroup season starts, the host and the LifeGroup leader will report to the LifeGroup coordinator as to how many attended their LifeGroups as a result of the previous month's Hangout sessions.

**Launch:**

We're launching this experiment August 2013.
