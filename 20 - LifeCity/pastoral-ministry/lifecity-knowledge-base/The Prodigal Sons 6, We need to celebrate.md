In Luke 15, Jesus tells three stories of something lost and something found: a lost sheep; a lost coin; and a lost son.  At the end of each story, Jesus describes a celebration.  The point is clear.  Jesus is happiest when the lost are found.  Jesus rejoices because he knows what awaits the saved. 
There is partying in heaven when one person is dead and now alive, lost but now found.
-"Just Like Jesus", Max Lucado
