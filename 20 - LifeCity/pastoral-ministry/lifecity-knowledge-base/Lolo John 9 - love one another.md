What is love?
- In our in most being, we long to love and to be loved. 
- We search for love and what love really means. 
- Pop culture: 
	- “Learning to love yourself is the greatest love of all” -Witney Houston 
	- “Love soft as an easy chair” -Salbakuta 
	- Movies: Love is when you feel the spark 
	- Love is defined with deep emotional feelings, sexual intimacy, compatibility 

If we don’t know the source of and real definition of love, we’re going to define and look for love in the wrong places.
- We’re going to define love through our own experiences 
	- If you experience abuse, you define love through abusive relationship 
	- If you’re exposed to media (which all of us are) 
		- We define love through the lenses of what we see 
		- Porn, violence, emotionalism  

Problem: Bad things happen when we look for and define love in the wrong places.
- Sometimes, you’re in love with the idea of love. 
- Married couples — you’re placing tremendous pressure with your spouse if you think you’ll going to find your idea of love in your spouse. Abuse happens. 
- Ladies — when we think that love is found in having a man in your life, you will find fulfillment and joy in what a man can offer you. What happens when men does not come up to your idea of love? Heartbreak happens. 
- Need need love unconditionally, pressure. 
- Leads to unhealthy relationships, abusive relationships. 
- We’re supposed to get the idea of love from our parents. But our parents fail us. We drag the same baggage in our adult life. 


**Love as defined by Lolo John**

Lolo John is Jesus’ best friend. And by being with him throughout his earthly ministry, he shared with us what love really means.
