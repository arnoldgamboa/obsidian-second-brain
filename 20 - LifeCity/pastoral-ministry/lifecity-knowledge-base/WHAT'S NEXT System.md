<b><u>DEFINITION:</u></b>  "What's Next" System is a process around the importance of doing our part as a Church in growing people who have just given their lives to Christ.

<b><u>THE WIN</u></b>**:** Seeing first time commitments to Christ take the "important steps".

<b><u>CONCERNED GROUP:</u></b> 

"What's Next" Follow Up team
Lead: Kuya Prudy - Care Pastor
Members: Arnold, Lead Pastor, Gerou, Joyce (or any of the ladies we feel ready to be part of the this group)

<b><u>BASIC STRATEGY</u></b>

People have been invited, we celebrated the decisions for Christ. But upon looking at the database, those who have committed their lives to Christ has not been attending and has long lost contact.

This basic process tries to resolve this issue through the 2 underlying principle: People needs to feel NEEDED and KNOWN -- it's all about relationships.

<b><u>STEPS</u></b>

Important steps
- Word, Prayer, Attend, Invite

Essential Steps
- LifeGroup, Baptism, Service, Mentorship

<b><u>PROCESS</u></b>

**1. SUNDAY EXPERIENCE**

- it will all start from the Sunday Experience. After the prayer of commitment, raised are hands, Arnold (or anyone doing the 180) will need to <b><u>emphasize</u></b> the request to fill out the communication card. 

- tell them to check the box under "MY DECISION TODAY that says "I'M COMMITING MY LIFE TO CHRIST'

- tell them how excited we are and that we want to give them a "What's Next" Kit to help them know the next steps in their journey with Jesus.

**2. MONDAY MORNING FOLLOW UP**

- Sort out the Communication Cards (K Prudy, or anyone assigned)

3 types of communication cards and what to do with these:
1. First time guests
	- Send an SMS that goes something like, "I'm happy to have seen you yesterday at LifeChurch. I hope you enjoyed your time with us."
	- To open a 'text conversation', ask "Where you given the book 'What on Earth am I Here For"? If the answer is "Yes", say, "I hope you'll love it. Do come back this Sunday". If answer is no, say "I'd love to give it to you personally this Sunday. Do come back".
1. Prayer requests
	- Usually sent by old time Lifers
	- Distribute the prayers to other members of the prayer team
	- Pray 
	- Send an SMS message telling them that you're praying for them.
1. Commitments to Christ
	- These are first time commitments who raised their hands
	- Send an SMS message that goes something like ***"We're so excited for your decision to give your life to Christ. I'd love to hear about your story (THIS IS THE "KNOWN" PART OF THE SMS) and hand you the "What's Next" Kit personally."***
	- Then say ***"Can you come earlier on Sunday"*** or, ***"Can we have a short chat after the Experience this Sunday?"***
	- The goal for this is 2 fold
	1. To implicitly invite them for next Sunday's Experience
	2. To talk to them about the "important steps"
 
**3. WHAT'S NEXT Chat**
- Make an appointment for a chat either before or after the Sunday Experience.
- Contents of the chat
- Know their story, etc.
	- Short gospel presentation (5 minutes)
	- Important steps -- Bible reading, Prayer, Sunday Experience attendance and inviting their friends
	- Discuss some of the essential steps:
		- Tell them about LifeGroups.
		- PLAY IT BY EAR: Baptism, Serving
- Connect them to LifeGroup leaders. Give her number to potential LifeGroup leader she can join to.
- HAND THEM THE WHAT'S NEXT KIT
	- Bible
	- A letter from the Lead Pastor
	- The "What's Next" booklet OR bookmark 
	- LC logo sticker
	- LC calling card
<b><u>OTHER INFORMATION</u></b>

- Be non-threathening as possible
- CONSISTENCY: 
- LIFEGROUPS (Weekly) - KNOWN
- VOLUNTEER VISION CASTING (Weekly) - NEEDED
- BAPTISM - every 3 months - KNOWN
- OPEN HOUSE - every 3 months (during LifeGroup break) - NEEDED, KNOWN
