**Welcome (*strictly* 4 minutes)**

- God is good all the time
- Welcome new guests (don’t say “visitors”. Use “guests”)
- Encourage them to fill out the connection card and the prayer card.
- Introduction to the message
- Pray.

Sample script:

God is good, all the time. And all the time, God is good!

I’d like to welcome everyone here at LifeCity Church. I hope that you are enjoying your time with us so far. If this is your first time, I’d like to specially welcome you. Please feel comfortable, grab a cup of coffee.

I’d like to ask you to pull out your connection card and fill it out. We’d like to you were here and connect with you. Also at the back of the card is our prayer card. We’d like to pray for you if you’ll let us know your burdens.

Today, we’ll continue our series “At The Movies”. We’re doing things differently during these 4 weeks. We’re trying to see spiritual truths in popular movies.

Without further ado, let’s now welcome our lead pastor, Arnold Gamboa. But before that, let’s have a quick prayer and welcome the Holy Spirit in our midst. (Pray…)

<b><u>Tithes and Offerings (3 mins)</u></b>

**-** celebrate those who gave their lives to Jesus
- what’s next kit
- tithes and offerings (1 to 1.5 mins)
- prayer for tithes and offerings

***Special note:***
*Make it snappy. Avoid comments regarding the message. Go straight to “Let’s celebrate for those who gave their lives to Christ…”* 

Sample script:

Let’s celebrate for those who gave their lives to Jesus! This is the reason why we do what we do. If you just gave your life to Jesus, we’d like to walk with you in this new journey. We’d like to give you what we call the “What’s Next Kit” available outside.

As we move to our worship through giving, let me share with you how giving has blessed me and has become part of my life. (Share some of the blessing — not just monetary blessings, but everything like health, relationship -- you received by being faithful in your giving)

I’d like to challenge you to do the same and worship God today through your tithes and offerings.

(Pray for tithes and offerings)
