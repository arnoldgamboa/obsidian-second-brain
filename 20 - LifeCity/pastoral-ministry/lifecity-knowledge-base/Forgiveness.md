<span style="color:#1d2129ff;">The main thing in forgiveness and mercy is this -- that when a Christian does this, God is shown to be who He really is, a merciful God and gloriously all-satisfying.</span>
<span style="color:#1d2129ff;">The ultimate reason for Christians to forgive and love those who hate them is to glorify God — to make Him look great in the eyes of man.</span>
