1. **It Depends On Who You Depend On** looks at how we can learn to depend more on God instead of material things for our happiness.
2. **How God Blesses Broken Hearts** teaches us how God uses our grief to draw us closer to him and to strengthen our character.
3. **The Strength of Gentleness** lists numerous benefits of learning to be gentle, and tells us how to add gentleness to our lives to be more like Christ.
4. **Why You Need to Stay Hungry** shows us why we must look to God and hunger for his righteousness to become fulfilled in life.
5. **Your Ministry of Mercy** tells us *why* we need to be merciful and *how* we can show mercy to others.
6. **God Blesses a Heart of Integrity** teaches us how to have integrity and build a reputation in our private lives that God will love.
7. **How to Reconcile a Relationship** gives us practical steps we can take to reconcile broken relationships in our lives.
8. **Handling Opposition to Your Faith** shows us how to stand strong in our faith when it's being questioned or attacked.
