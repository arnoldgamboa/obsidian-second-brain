1. The pursuit of perfection - God doesn’t demand our perfection; he wants our surrender.
2. The curse of comparison - Comparison either makes you feel superior or inferior. Neither honors God.
3. The burden of sin. Sin promises satisfaction but robs us of peace and intimacy with God
4. The love of self. It’s easy to focus on ourselves, but we find our lives when we lose them by serving Jesus and giving our lives for others
5. Fear of the future and regret of the past. - We can’t change our past or control our future, but we can surrender what happened and trust God with what is to come.
