**A. Position description**

The "music coordinator", in direct coordination with the Directional Leadership Team and in agreement with the vision to “become a church people far from God love to attend”, will lead the music team to become an integral part of making the church’s vision become a reality. As a music coordinator, your primarily role is to make sure that the music team is aligned with the vision.

**B. Specific roles**

1. Guide the music team towards the specific musical sound the leadership team envisioned 
2. Provide musical direction during rehearsals 
3. Manage and balance the team roster on a week-to-week/month-to-month basis. This includes assigning each member of the team on particular weeks at least a month ahead of time. 
	1. If there are conflicts in the schedule, the music coordinator should be able to reassess the roster and realign other team member’s schedule 
	2. If possible, the music coordinator should also be able source out that which is lacking from outside the team
