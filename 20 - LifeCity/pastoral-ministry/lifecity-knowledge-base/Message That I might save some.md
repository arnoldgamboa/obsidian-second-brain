<u>YOU</u>

(One liner)
**FOR THE SAKE OF THE GOSPEL, SAVE SOME.**

Questions to ask yourself today:

HOW CAN I BE INCLUSIVE “TO SAVE SOME”?
- Christians doesn’t need to be isolated. We just need to be insulated.
- We don’t isolate ourselves from our friends “to be holy”. We be with them, but we need to be “insulated” with our relationship with God, thus protecting us from sinning.
- “Do anything short of sin to reach people who don’t know Christ”.
- sight some examples

WHAT CAN I DO TO “SAVE SOME”?
- start praying for your friends this year
- listen to them, talk to them, counsel them, have coffee with them
- bring them to LifeGroup

- bring them to LifeCity


WE
- LifeCity is designed with you and your friends in mind. 
- we try to be as Christ centered as possible (because only Christ changes lives) and welcoming as possible. We’ll try our best to make you be proud of LifeCity so you can bring your friends here.
- we will not be a “clique”. This is not just about us, it’s about people out there who needs Jesus. <u>(note on this, practical application you can use: Once when I was talking with Clark about the issues we encountered with Honey/Clark’s mom, it dawned on me that the music team has a “sub-clique” (Clark, Kate, Honey, Mikey). We must consistently reject it, be aware that it’s our natural tendency, and correct it as soon as we see it happen)</u>
