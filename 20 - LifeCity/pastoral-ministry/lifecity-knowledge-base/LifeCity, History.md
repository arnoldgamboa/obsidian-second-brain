1. Pre-church plan days
	1. Through the ministry we’re involved with, somebody introduced us to “The Purpose Driven Church.
	2. We are quickly are drawn to it. the concepts of doing church is different
	3. Since then, we started praying to someday plant a church with purpose
1. Purpose Driven Life small groups
	1. When the “Purpose Driven Life” book was launched, we purchased the materials that will help us minister to our friends.
	2. We purchased the CD’s and workbooks
	3. We started conducting small group meetings using the Purpose Driven Life strategy 
	4. Because of this, we saw lives given to Jesus. We connected with fellow Christians also as a result
1. Church plant 
	1. Because of the new lives ministered through the PDL groups and connections with Christian friends, we decided to plant a church similar to the Purpose Driven Church paradigm
	2. We called it Grace Place Metro Manila
	3. Through many challenges, we saw lives that are changed by the gospel.
	4. From the leadership stand point, Pastor Rick Warren’s ministry and Saddleback Church has been a great help and influence to us. 
	5. We we preach sermons from [Pastors.com](http://Pastors.com).
	6. We purchased materials that we would eventually use for the church like the “Foundations”, teaching and workbook series on the Da Vinci Code and The Passion of the Christ when it went out, and other resources. 
	7. We’ve attended Pastor Rick’s Purpose Driven Church conference in Manila when he visited years ago.
1. LifeChurch Makati
	1. Upon partnership with Craig Groeschel’s LifeChurch in Oklahoma, where we regularly stream his messages every week, we renamed the church as “LifeChurch Makati”
	2. About 3 years after, we decided to to produce our own weekly content and preach live.
	3. We renamed the church “LifeCity Church”
1. LifeCity Church
	1. Today, after 12 years of ministry, from renting a hotel function room on a weekly basis, we are now renting a permanent location in the middle of a “harvest field” — a location where young, urban families are located.
	2. We are praying to make the newly renovated hall to be a center for the community for activities that benefits not just the church but also the people that we want to minister to. 
1. Our Dream for LifeCity
	1. We plan to offer the place for free for the community 
	2. On weekdays, it can be converted into a co-working space for parents who work from home (as a result of workplace changes during the pandemic). We want it to be a place where they can temporarily find relief and find a quiet place to work.
	3. We pray for the opportunity to provide family seminars, after school activities for the kids and other things that can benefit the community. We do this the let them know the love of Jesus can be felt and seen through his Church in the most practical way.
