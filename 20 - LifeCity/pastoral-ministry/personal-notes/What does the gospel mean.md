<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">What is the gospel?</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">Gospel</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">Latin: evangelium</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">“Good news”</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">If there’s good news, there’s the bad news prior to that.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">God</span>
- <span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">Holy and Perfect (does not tolerate anything lower than perfection/holiness)</span> 
- <span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#191919ff;">Creator (center of the universe)</span> 

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#191919ff;">To the Lord your God belong the heavens, even the highest heavens, the earth and everything in it.</span> <span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">Deut 10:14</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">Sin</span>
- <span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#191919ff;">God created man for his glory</span> 
- <span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#191919ff;">Man and God lived in perfect relationship (perfect sabbath)</span> 
- <span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#191919ff;">God specifically gave man free will as unique element of relationship</span> 
- <span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">Sin entered the relationship and disrupted the “Sabbath” rest man has with God</span> 
- <span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#191919ff;">Become of this, we are cursed to be eternally separated with God</span> 
	- <span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#191919ff;">For the wages of sin is death (Romans 6:23a)</span> 
- <span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#191919ff;">We are born with this nature</span> 

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#191919ff;">I do not understand what I do. For what I want to do I do not do, but what I hate I do. Romans 7:15</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#191919ff;">Jesus</span> 

- <span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">God, in his love for mankind, solves the problem we ourselves created.</span>  
- <span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">Jesus, the 2nd Person in the Godhead, became man and died on the cross to pay for the penalty of our separation from God</span> 

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">"And you were dead in the trespasses and sins in which you once walked, following the course of this world, following the prince of the power of the air, the spirit that is now at work in the sons of disobedience—among whom we all once lived in the passions of our flesh, carrying out the desires of the body and the mind, and were by nature children of wrath, like the rest of mankind. But God, being rich in mercy, because of the great love with which he loved us, even when we were dead in our trespasses, made us alive together with Christ—by grace you have been saved— and raised us up with him and seated us with him in the heavenly places in Christ Jesus, so that in the coming ages he might show the immeasurable riches of his grace in kindness toward us in Christ Jesus.” (Eph 2:1-7)</span>

- <span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">Jesus saved me from “drowning” (I was helpless, but God…”)</span>
