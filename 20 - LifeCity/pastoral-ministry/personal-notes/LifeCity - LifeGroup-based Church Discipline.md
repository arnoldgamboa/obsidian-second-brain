<span style="font-size:15.428571701049805pt;"><b>What church discipline is for</b></span>

- <span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">Those who are living in sin</span> 
- <span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">Those who are trouble-makers who sow discord (Rom. 16:17)</span> 
- <span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">Those who deny and/or disobey the doctrines of faith (2 Thes. 3:13-14; 1 Tim 6:3-4)</span> 

<span style="font-size:15.428571701049805pt;"><b>LifeGroup-based church disciple process</b></span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">When a LifeGroup member is living in sin, sowing discord or in disobedience to church doctrines:</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">1. Confront privately. (Matt. 18:15)</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">If he admits and repents:</span>
- <span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">If in church leadership, he is removed from leadership; LifeGroup Leader and  Pastors determines restoration plan.</span> 
- <span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">Will undergo counseling with LifeGroup leader</span> 

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">2. If no admission happens, take 2 witnesses and go and plead with him again. (Matt 18:16). If successful, same rehabilitation process as #1.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">3. If still no admission or confession, inform the members of LifeGroup (Matt 18:16). Pray for the person. Enjoin him to confess, repent, and turn from sin.  If he refuses, “hand him to the devil” (1 Cor 5:5). Meaning, treat him as a non-believer. Still welcome to the group, will receive the same love but there’s no expectation.</span>

<span style="font-size:15.428571701049805pt;"><b>Purpose of Discipline</b></span>

1. <span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">For growth in faith (discipleship)</span> 
2. <span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">To maintain standards of the church to a watching world (Matt. 5:13-16)</span> 
3. <span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">Help the guilty person find their way to God (restoration) (2 Cor. 2:6-8)</span> 
4. <span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">To escape God’s judgment upon habitually sinning saints (1 Cor. 11:30)</span>
