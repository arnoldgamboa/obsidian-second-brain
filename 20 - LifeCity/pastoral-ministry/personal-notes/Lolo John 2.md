<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">Problem: Denial of sin</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">1 John 8</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">8If we say we have no sin, we deceive ourselves, and the truth is not in us.</span> 
- <span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">we are in denial</span> 
- <span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">we want to believe what we’re doing is not a sin</span> 

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">Solution: Confession</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">9If we confess our sins, he is faithful and just to forgive us our sins and to cleanse us from all unrighteousness.</span> 

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">“confess” - homologeo, to say the same thing, to agree.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">“our”, not in the original greek. it may mean “the”</span>
- <span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">will ask my kids to bring me an apple, or bring me THE apple</span> 
- <span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">be specific when asking God for forgiveness</span> 

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">“all unrighteousness” - everything else. God’s wants restored fellowship, intimacy with Him</span>



<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">Danger with confession - abuse</span>
- <span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">that’s not God’s plan</span> 
- <span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">repeat offender</span>
