I was applying for an IT Director job and this company seamed to have been very interested with me. I’ve gone though the whole interview process and it felt like, and I’m confident that, I will be hired. 

Until it didn’t. 

A few days before I received the disappointment, God must have led me to a series of things to read. Articles, blog posts — all seemed to say that I should not take it personally when I get a job rejection.  That I should keep my self esteem intact. 

God cares even in those small details in my life. 

When I received the news I was ready. And I was able to redirect the failure into trust in Gods plan. I used the experience to get me closer to him.
