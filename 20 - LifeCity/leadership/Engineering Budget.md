**Engineering Team Budget for Fiscal Year 2024-2025**
I. Personnel Costs

- &nbsp;
	- **Salaries**
	- Software Engineers: $X (number of engineers x average salary)
	- QA Engineers: $Y (number of QA engineers x average salary)
	- DevOps: $Z (number of DevOps engineers x average salary)
	- Team Lead/Management: $W (number of leads/managers x average salary)
	- **Benefits**
	- Health Insurance: $A
	- Retirement Contributions: $B
	- Others (e.g., training, conferences): $C
**Total Personnel Costs:** $(X + Y + Z + W + A + B + C)
II. Operational Costs

- &nbsp;
	- **Office Space and Utilities**
	- Rent: $D per month
	- Utilities (Internet, Electricity, etc.): $E per month
	- **Software Licenses and Tools**
	- Development Tools (IDEs, Git, etc.): $F
	- Project Management Tools (JIRA, Trello, etc.): $G
	- Communication Tools (Slack, Email, etc.): $H
	- **Hardware and Equipment**
	- Computers and Monitors: $I
	- Servers/Cloud Services: $J
	- Miscellaneous (keyboards, mice, etc.): $K
**Total Operational Costs:** $(D + E + F + G + H + I + J + K)
III. Project-Specific Costs

- &nbsp;
	- **Development Costs**
	- External Consultants/Contractors: $L
	- Specific Software Licenses for Projects: $M
	- **Testing and Quality Assurance**
	- Testing Tools and Services: $N
	- External QA Services: $O
	- **Training and Development**
	- Workshops and Courses: $P
	- Certifications: $Q
**Total Project-Specific Costs:** $(L + M + N + O + P + Q)
IV. Contingency Fund
- **Contingency (5-10% of Total Costs):** $R
Total Annual Budget:
**$(Total Personnel Costs + Total Operational Costs + Total Project-Specific Costs + Contingency Fund)**
This budget template is designed to cover all the major areas of expenditure for a software engineering team. The specific amounts (represented by X, Y, Z, etc.) will vary based on your company's size, location, and specific needs. It's also important to consider that this budget should be flexible to accommodate changes and unforeseen expenses throughout the year.
