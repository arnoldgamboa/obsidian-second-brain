Absolutely! Let's dive into the essential modules and components to create a successful HRIS app for the Philippine market. Here's a breakdown of the core areas, along with some unique aspects to consider:
**Must-Have Modules**
1. **Core Employee Data Management:**
	- Complete employee profiles (contact info, demographics, job history, dependents, emergency contacts)
	- Document Storage (contracts, government forms like SSS, PhilHealth, Pag-IBIG, etc.)
	- Organizational charts and customizable reporting for a clear view of company structure.
1. **Attendance and Time Tracking:**
	- Options for multiple tracking methods (biometrics, web-based, mobile app).
	- Integration with Philippine-specific overtime and holiday calculation rules.
	- Flexible shift scheduling and leave management.
1. **Payroll Processing:**
	- Automated calculations based on Philippine tax regulations, deductions, and government contributions (SSS, PhilHealth, Pag-IBIG).
	- Generation of government-mandated reports (e.g., BIR forms).
	- Support for various payment methods (bank transfers, e-wallets, etc.)
	- Generation of clear payslips for employees.
1. **Compliance:**
	- Automated reminders for expiring contracts, licenses, and certifications.
	- Built-in compliance with Philippine labor laws and regulations.
	- Customizable reporting to support audits.
**Highly Desirable Features**
1. **Recruitment & Onboarding:**
	- Applicant tracking system (ATS) to manage job postings, resumes, and interview scheduling.
	- Smooth onboarding process with digital forms and signatures for a paperless experience.
1. **Performance Management:**
	- Goal setting and tracking aligned with company KPIs.
	- Customizable performance review templates with a focus on both results and development.
1. **Training & Development:**
	- Course management system to organize in-house or external training programs.
	- Tracking of training attendance and certifications earned.
1. **Employee Engagement & Self-Service**
	- Internal communication tools (company announcements, polls, surveys).
	- Employee portal for requesting time off, viewing payslips, updating personal information, and accessing company policies.
**Key Considerations for the Philippine Market**
- **Cloud-Based Solution:** SaaS (Software as a Service) models are gaining popularity with Philippine companies, offering scalability and affordability.
- **Mobile First:** Ensure a seamless and intuitive mobile app experience given the widespread smartphone usage in the Philippines.
- **Government Regulations:** Maintain strict adherence to ever-evolving labor laws and government contribution calculations.
- **Data Privacy:** Comply with the Philippines' Data Privacy Act to safeguard sensitive employee information.
- **Multi-Language Support (Optional):** Consider Tagalog/Filipino language versions to broaden your reach.
**Additional Selling Points:**
- **Intuitive User Interface:** Provide an easy-to-use design that caters to HR teams of various sizes and technical expertise.
- **Excellent Customer Support:** Offer strong local support and training resources.
- **Integrations:** Ability to integrate with popular accounting, timekeeping, or communication software used in the Philippines.
