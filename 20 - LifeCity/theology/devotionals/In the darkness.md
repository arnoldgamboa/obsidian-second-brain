If we say we have fellowship with him while we walk in darkness, we lie and do not practice the truth. But if we walk in the light, as he is in the light, we have fellowship with one another, and the blood of Jesus his Son cleanses us from all sin.”

O
God is holy. Sinless. He does not tolerate sin. We on the other hand are sinners. Sometimes, we claim that we have in fellowship with God but walks in the opposition to God’s holiness. This is hypocrisy. 

A
Verse 7 is application for me. If I want to repair my fellowship with God, I need to be authentic and admit my sins. 

P
Lord, help me walk in the light and be ready to admit my sins every time I commit it.
