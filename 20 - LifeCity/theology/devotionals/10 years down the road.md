Rick Warren said:

Ask yourself these four questions:

1) What do I want to be? 
I want to become a big influence in the market place for Jesus.

2) What do I want to do? 
I want to serve God full time in the local church.

3) What do I want to have? 
I want to have the time and resources to support my family a comfortable living and work full time in the local church.

4) Why do I want it? 
That's how God wired me for a his glory.
