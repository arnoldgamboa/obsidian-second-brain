Focus on the eternal than on temporal. 

In my decisions, do I just consider the here and now or do I consider the eternal? In my future plans, do I just consider my comfort or do I also consider my growth as a follower of Jesus? In everything I do everyday, does it magnify "the things above" than the "things on earth?"
