Simple truth: if you love Jesus, you need to love your brothers too. 

It's the ultimate test of our love of God. I think that we can only declare our love of God up to the level of hate we have with our brothers. Meaning, the people we love the least represents the level of love we have for God. Why? Because it's easy to say I love an invisible Being. But what we believe to be true for an invisible Being is reflected with our actions for visible ones. 

I pray that God will grow me in knowledge of His love so I can love my fellowmen more.
