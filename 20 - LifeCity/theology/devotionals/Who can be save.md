Jesus was talking to the rich young ruler asking about how to be saved. Jesus said, be perfect and give away all your possessions. He left disappointed because he can't do what Jesus asked him to do. The disciples then said that no one can be saved if that is the standard. And Jesus said, with man it is impossible. Salvation is only possible with God. 

It's 2001 all over for me as God reminded me of the very same things that made me fall in love with Jesus -- salvation by grace through faith. In Christ alone, not through my good works, can I enter the presence of God in eternity. It's great to be able to go back to basics and be reminded why am doing what I'm doing and who the foundation of my life has been and always.
