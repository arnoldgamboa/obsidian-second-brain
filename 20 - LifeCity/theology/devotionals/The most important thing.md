It is very easy to be overwhelmed and be excited with what's happening in the ministry. We think that it's the best thing that can happen in our relationship with God. You think that seeing people come to Jesus is the best thing that can happen to us. Leaders are wired to think that way. 

But we should go back to the very essence of what we're doing.  We should remember the very reason why we're doing what we're doing as followers of Jesus as pastors and leaders of the church is because in the first place we are saved by Jesus. That we have now a place in heaven. That Jesus has done the hard part of the equation.

The only reason that we can rejoice because of the fact that Jesus gave us a place in heaven. That is where our focus should be. That is where the essence of our ministry should be based on. That is where our energy should flow from. Jesus is the very essence of what we do.

Lord, thank you for reminding me over and over again the season in my life that Jesus should be the focus of everything that I do. Thank you for rescuing me from myself. Please allow me to serve you based on the overflow of my relationship with Jesus.
