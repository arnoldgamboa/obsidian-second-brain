Matthew 8:2-3 NIV

A man with leprosy came and knelt before him and said, “Lord, if you are willing, you can make me clean.”  Jesus reached out his hands nd and touched the man. “I am willing,” he said. “Be clean!” Immediately he was cleansed of his leprosy.

Are you in the 'if you are willing' moment?

Sometimes when we pray, if we're honest enough, you'll notice that we are actually in doubt whether God will answer our prayers positively or not. And sometimes our doubts are legitimate. Our prayers are selfish and clearly out of God's will. But we pray anyway. *Baka* *makalusot.*

However, sometimes, our prayers are not selfish and it honors God but we still doubt if God is willing to do it. It is a legitimate concern. We ask why He is not answering. We think God might have other plans.

But in the middle of your 'if you're willing' moment, remember that Jesus is listening. He might be willing to answer your prayer positively. Our not. But he knows your concerns. He listens. And his plans are far better than ours. He's not sovereign for nothing in the first place.
