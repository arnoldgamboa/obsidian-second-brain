Matthew 3:11 NIV

“I baptize you with water for repentance. But after me comes one who is more powerful than I, w**hose sandals I am not worthy to carry.** He will baptize you with the Holy Spirit and fire.."

As a leader, the temptation is ever more powerful to get credit to whatever success we experience even how small it is.

I always need to be reminded that it's all about Jesus. My role is to prepare the way.  I need to point people to Jesus not to me or to the things I do.

Lord, this Easter morning, may you become the center of everything that I do at your church and in my family. Please let me be the leader you want me to become - - a leader that points people to you every single day.
