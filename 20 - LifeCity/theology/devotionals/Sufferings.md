Sufferings > endurance > character > hope > manifestation of Gods love
While sufferings are not in anyway something that I rejoice about, but it should be welcomed as a means to produce endurance that builds my character and hope which the love of God manifests. I guess it's Gods natural process of building my character. 

When I suffer, I need to remind myself that there is more to it than just pain.
