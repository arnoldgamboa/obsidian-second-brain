Context here Paul's discussion of the old and new covenant. Now that Jesus is our covenant with God, we are free. Free from what? The bondage of the law that condemns. 

If there is a time when I feel that God condemns me for breaking the law, may the Spirit remind me that I'm free from such -- that my obedience is not for God's approval but FROM his approval. I obey because I'm in a relationship not to be able to be in a relationship with God! Such a great security of joy, trust and love from God.
