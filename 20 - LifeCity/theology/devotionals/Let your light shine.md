Matthew 5:16 NIV

In the same way, let your light shine before others, that they may see your good deeds and glorify your Father in heaven.

Jesus said,  I am the light of the world. If I have Christ, I will need to let him shine in everything I do in such a way that the glory goes back to the Father.

2 difficulties :

1. Hiding the light. It's hard to be bold and let people know about my faith. It's a constant struggle.

2. Pride. When something wonderful happens, it's a big temptation to grab the lime light and not let the glory bunch back to God.

Jesus,  you are my light. Let me shine through you. Let me be a close representation of who you really are. And when that happens,  may my pride give way to the real owner of glory,  You.
