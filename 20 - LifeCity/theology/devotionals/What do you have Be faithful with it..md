What do you have? Money? Talents? Opportunities? Influence?

However little it is right now make sure that you Use it to the best of your ability because you will answer for it in the throne of God. 

Don't expect to have more if you don't value your little. Expect God to multiply what you have if you are able to steward whatever he entrusted you.
