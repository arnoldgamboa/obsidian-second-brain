David has a chance to kill Saul but he didn't. He spared him, a guy who wants him dead, and provided grace he does not deserve.

This story, in a larger context, represent the grace of God towards us -- and me. I offend God, I sinned against him. But he spared me from his wrath. He provided grace I didn't deserve.
