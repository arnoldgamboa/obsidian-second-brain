“Esau said, "I am about to die; of what use is a birthright to me?" Jacob said, "Swear to me now." So he swore to him and sold his birthright to Jacob. Then Jacob gave Esau bread and lentil stew, and he ate and drank and rose and went his way. Thus Esau despised his birthright.”

This story is a powerful illustration on how we sell our birthright everyday. Our relationship with Jesus suffers when we give in to our desires. One simple mistake radiates to a lifetime of suffering. 

This should be a reminder to me, in a lot of ways. I should be very careful with my dealings, with the relationships that I build and the potential problems it create. I should be proactive and not allow anything to even be closer that will break the heart of my family, my church and my God.
