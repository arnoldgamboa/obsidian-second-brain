And when it was day, he departed and went into a desolate place. (Luke 4:42a) https://bible.com/59/luk.4.42.esv

“desolate” (adj) -  (of a place) deserted of people and in a state of bleak and dismal emptiness.

As it is Jesus’ custom, he starts the day to be alone, be empty and and a cup of coffee. (Or maybe not the coffee part). I can imagine that Jesus re-energized himself with His father’s presence each day.

Thank you Holy Spirit for this moment. For energizing me with your presence.
