Paul is still in the subject of giving. I don't believe that this verse makes God a vending machine. And I certainly do not believe in the prosperity gospel. I do think that this verse though shows that God is he one who invented the rule of sowing and reaping. What you sow, you reap. And in the aspect of giving, if we sow generosity, God will develop in us a generous heart. 

What am I sowing today? Am I being intentional in my generosity? May God continue to develop a generous heart in me.
