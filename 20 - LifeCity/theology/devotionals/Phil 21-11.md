O
Self-love. A very popular concept. While accepting who you are (in Christ) is very important, accepting who we are not also is important. By default, because we’re sinners, we are not humble and selfless. We look for our own interests. Paul implores us to do the opposite. We need to ‘do nothing out of selfish ambition or vain conceit’ (selfish pride). Instead of looking for our own interest, look to the interests of others. Instead of being ‘me’ focused, be ‘you’ focused.

A
There are times where I’m being too self-absorbed. I have to remind myself — it’s not all about me. The world does not revolve around me. And then serve others instead — my wife, my kids, my family, the church.
