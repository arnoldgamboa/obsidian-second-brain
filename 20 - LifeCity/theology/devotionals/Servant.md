Scripture
“But it shall not be so among you. But whoever would be great among you must be your servant, and whoever would be first among you must be slave of all. For even the Son of Man came not to be served but to serve, and to give his life as a ransom for many."”
‭‭Mark‬ ‭10:43-45‬ ‭ESV‬‬

Observation 
James and John’s (or even probably everyone in that room) tendency is be focused on self — which came from pride and arrogance. Jesus lovingly taught and redirected them. He gave them an example: himself. He came not to be served but to serve. That’s the essence of humility and true leadership. 

Application
As a leader in my family and community, it is my natural tendency to seek approval which leads to pride. The spiritual way to combat this sinful tendency is service. Serve first before looking to be served. 

Prayer
Lord, thank you for reminding me today that my pride is getting in the way of my spiritual growth. Allow me to serve your people wholeheartedly which will hopefully make me a better follower of your Son.
