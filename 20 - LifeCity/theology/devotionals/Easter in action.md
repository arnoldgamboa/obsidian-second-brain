“For it is better to suffer for doing good, if that should be God’s will, than for doing evil. For Christ also suffered once for sins, the righteous for the unrighteous, that he might bring us to God, being put to death in the flesh but made alive in the spirit,”
‭‭1 Peter‬ ‭3:17-18‬ ‭ESV‬‬
http://bible.com/59/1pe.3.17-18.esv

It's Easter Sunday and my scheduled devotional text pointed to this. :)

I call this Easter in action. This verse represents how Christ suffered for the good and came out victorious. 

As the texts says, when confronted by a choice to suffer by doing good or suffering by doing evil, choose the obvious better choice. Because in life, it is a fact that I will suffer, while living in this sin sick world. When confronted by choices, I pray that the Holy Spirit will empower me to choose the right thing, even if it means suffering because of it. I know, while at it, I will be given the strength to endure.
