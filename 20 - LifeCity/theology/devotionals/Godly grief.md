“As it is, I rejoice, not because you were grieved, but because you were grieved into repenting. For you felt a godly grief, so that you suffered no loss through us. For godly grief produces a repentance that leads to salvation without regret, whereas worldly grief produces death.”
‭‭2 Corinthians‬ ‭7:9-10‬ ‭ESV‬‬

Paul has been rebuking the church in Corinth of different sins. And in the middle of these rebukes he reminds them that he knows they are offended by these. But it's alright to be offended because it leads to repentance that leads to salvation without regret. It's better than worldly grief -- a grief that is not based on conviction from the Holy Spirit but comes from the consequences of their actions. Paul said it leads to death. 

2 things. 1) I should be open to corrections. It will hurt, but should lead me to repentance. 2) it's not my personality, but out of love to people around me, especially those that are followers of Chrisf, I should be bold in calling out sins and walk them through repentance. This is the messy job of a pastor. 

Lord, make me vulnerable to corrections and lead me to repentance. Give me boldness that loves the sinners and pastor them into repentance.
