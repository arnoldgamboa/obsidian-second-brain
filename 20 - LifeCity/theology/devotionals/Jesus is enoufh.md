“But whatever gain I had, I counted as loss for the sake of Christ. Indeed, I count everything as loss because of the surpassing worth of knowing Christ Jesus my Lord. For his sake I have suffered the loss of all things and count them as rubbish, in order that I may gain Christ”
‭‭Philippians‬ ‭3:7-8‬ ‭ESV‬‬

Everything I have, all the knowledge or wealth or profession or relationships -- all are rubbish compared to what I have in Christ. During my early days as a follower of Jesus, I have my mantra: "Jesus is enough". 

When things get though, Jesus is enough. When things goes well, I should not dwell on it because Jesus is enough. Every thing is all about Jesus.
