Patience. Urgh!

The picture of a farmer patiently waiting for the plant to grow. It’s a perfect illustration of patience. Patience recognizes that time is required to process things, to grow and to bear fruit.

Patience is hard to develop. I’m having a hard time with patience. And it’s because patience is developed through trusting God. At the root of it all, I need to trust God with everything in my life.
