Matthew 26:35 NLT

“No!” Peter insisted. “Even if I have to die with you, I will never deny you!” And all the other disciples vowed the same.

Peter promised. He failed. 
Jesus' promise, however, is not broken.

I can't trust myself. But I can trust Jesus.  I will eventually fail him, but he would never give up on me.

Thank you Lord for your grace. Thank you for loving me unconditionally.
