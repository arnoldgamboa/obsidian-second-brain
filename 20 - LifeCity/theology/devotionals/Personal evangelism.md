“When they deliver you over, do not be anxious how you are to speak or what you are to say, for what you are to say will be given to you in that hour. For it is not you who speak, but the Spirit of your Father speaking through you.”
‭‭Matthew‬ ‭10:19-20‬ ‭ESV‬‬

The apostles were sent to proclaim the word of God. Jesus charged them and told them to not fear if they would not know what to say. The Holy Spirit will speak through them. 

Today's reading is so relevant as the church is currently studying personal evangelism. As if God made it a point today that we are in the right track. The common issue that I encounter about telling Jesus' love to others is my inacapability to say the right things. By Jesus is encouraging me today -- "just be available, you will be my mouthpiece."
