The story of David and Bathsheba is an interesting case study of man's sinfulness. However, this story is different from most movie plots. In this story, we see redemption. We see
God himself calling out the sin, David repented, forgiven, and yet also saw the consequence of his sin thought th death of his son. 

Stories like this are given so that we -- and myself included, will see my own sinful desires and before it even happen should be warned of the consequences. Man's sin nature is not hard to decode. It's been there for centuries. There's no way I shouldn't know mine and react according to God's holiness before it even happens.
