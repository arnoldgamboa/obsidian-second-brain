Jesus has one formula against temptation: watch and pray. Don't trust my willingness to resist, or my perceived spiritual strength. Because the spirit might be willing, but the flesh is weak. 

Craig G calls this "margin". I need to realize that I am weak and being watchful -- or being self aware -- coupled with prayer, is my margin against falling into Satans trap.
