These verses are from Jesus rebuke to the Pharisees.  But this can well be a good for of evaluation even for a Christian. It is easy to say the right things but it's the heart that matters -- it's that posture towards, or away from God that He sees and evaluate.

Reflection time is a good time for me to evaluate myself where my heart is postured towards. I may say all the right things but I need to see where my heart is going after -- is it God or is it my own personal desire?
