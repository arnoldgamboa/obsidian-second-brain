For where your treasure is, there your heart will be also.”

It's so easy to be carried away by the worries of this life -- career, relationships, money, travel, fun. While these are definitely not a bad thing in itself, the real question is, where is my heart postured towards? Is it towards eternity or towards the things of this current life? 

What am I doing today, what are my plans fir the future that puts the things in this life above my heart for eternity?
