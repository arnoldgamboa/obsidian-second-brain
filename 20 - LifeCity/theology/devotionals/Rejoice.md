“Rejoice in the Lord always. I will say it again: Rejoice!”
‭‭Philippians‬ ‭4:4‬ ‭NIV‬‬
<a href="https://www.bible.com/111/php.4.4.niv" rel="noopener" class="external-link" target="_blank" style="color:#00eeff;"><u>https://www.bible.com/111/php.4.4.niv</u></a>

Rejoice - it’s hard to do when things are not going well. 
In the Lord - rejoicing is possible not through riches or relationships or circumstances but in the Lord
Always - rejoicing in the Lord is possible, always. Even when things are not going well. Even if things doesn’t go my way. Because of the presence of God, it is possible to rejoice. 

I’m in a season when things are busy, things are hard. Not a bad thing, but rejoicing needs to be present. When I miss my time with God, I’m “gone with the wind” of my busy schedule. I need to be reminded to slow down and rejoice in the Lord, always. 

Lord, thank you for this moment of contemplation. Thank you that I can rejoice because of you. Because you’re with me in good times and in bad. Please remind me always to slow down when I forget to appreciate your goodness.
