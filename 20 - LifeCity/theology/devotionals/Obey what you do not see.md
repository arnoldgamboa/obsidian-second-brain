Matthew 1:24 NIV

When Joseph woke up, he did what the angel of the Lord had commanded him and took Mary home as his wife.

God will ask for the impossible. Sometimes the hardest thing, even the most uncomfortable thing to do.

If you are very sure that God is the one speaking -- personally or through his messengers, feel the tension and let God win. Obey.

Sometimes if not most of the times I fail in this. Its easy to obey if the task is simple to do. That's pretty basic. But if it requires to lay down my ego or a little bit of humility, it certainly is hard to obey.

However through the power of Gods spirit, he empowers me to follow the impossible if done though my own strength.

Jesus, please forgive me for being stubborn at times. Please allow me to know when you're asking me to follow and obey. And then give me the unnatural power to obey when it is impossible to obey through my personal strength.
