“But God chose what is foolish in the world to shame the wise; God chose what is weak in the world to shame the strong; God chose what is low and despised in the world, even things that are not, to bring to nothing things that are, so that no human being might boast in the presence of God.

so that, as it is written, “Let the one who boasts, boast in the Lord.””
‭‭1 Corinthians‬ ‭1:27-29, 31‬ ‭ESV‬‬
http://bible.com/59/1co.1.27-29,31.esv

I am not wise, not strong, not somebody. I have nothing to boast about. If there's anything I should boast about, it's God who makes everything possible. 

This truth doesn't just apply to me when seen by other people. This also applies to me when talking to myself. I need to always remind myself that whatever I have, may it be possessions or knowledge or skill is anything but mine. It's Gods. He gives, he can take it away anytime. This truth will and should make me humble and depend on God every single day of my life.
