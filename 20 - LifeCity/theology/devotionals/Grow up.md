The context of this verse is Paul's counsel against speaking in tongues in the church. But this is also a good counsel that can be applied in my daily life. 

This verse got me thinking about two things: 1) which aspects of my life am I being immature in the way I think? How does thinking with mature minds look like, and 2) what does being infants in evil mean? Infants being weak and does not have a good grip of their own decision making, does that mean being easily swayed to things not of God is being "infant in evil"?

These are questions worth contemplating about throughout the day. Whatever the answers are, I should desire to grow in my faith everyday.
