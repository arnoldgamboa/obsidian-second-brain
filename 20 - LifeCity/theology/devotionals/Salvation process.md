Gods process for one's salvation -- sent to preach, heard the good news, believed, call on him by faith, salvation. 

It all starts with someone being sent by God to reach out to someone and sharing the gospel. That's why I have to understand my calling -- that God sent me to where I am today to create an impact in the lives of people in such a way that they'll hear the grace and love of Christ that will lead them to believe and accept Him for salvation. Thank you Lord for this opportunity to be part of the process.
