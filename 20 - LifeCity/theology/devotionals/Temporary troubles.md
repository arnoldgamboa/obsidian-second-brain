For this light momentary affliction is preparing for us an eternal weight of glory beyond all comparison, as we look not to the things that are seen but to the things that are unseen. For the things that are seen are transient, but the things that are unseen are eternal. (2 Cor. 4:17-18)

It is always tempting to be satisfied  with the here and now. The things that are seen today seems more appealing than that is not seen. When troubles, trials and temptations come, giving in to it is easier. But I have to look beyond the here and now to eternity. These troubles will pass away eventually and what lies beyond eternity is so worth the pain and sacrifices. 

When I’m tempted, when I suffer, when I feel like giving up — I will always remember : the things that are seen is temporary, the things unseen is eternal.

Lord, please give me strength that comes only from you so I can be strong for the here and now and able to look beyond.
