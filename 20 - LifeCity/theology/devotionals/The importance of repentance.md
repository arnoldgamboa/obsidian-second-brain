“For day and night your hand was heavy upon me; my strength was dried up as by the heat of summer. I acknowledged my sin to you, and I did not cover my iniquity; I said, "I will confess my transgressions to the Lord," and you forgave the iniquity of my sin.”
‭‭Psalms‬ ‭32:4-5‬ ‭ESV‬‬
http://bible.com/59/psa.32.4-5.esv

Sins weigh me down. And when I'm in sin and has not repented of it, it sure does dry up my strength. 

But God forgives. When I confess my sins and trusts God with it in repentance, the weigh is lifted up off me. 

Important note about repentance: it should be told to God and a trusted follower of Christ. That is for accountability. This is God's design for community.
