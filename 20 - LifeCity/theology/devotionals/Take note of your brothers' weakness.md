Paul was talking about eating food served to idols. He said it's just ok. But if your brother sees you and he stumbles, it's better to just not eat for the sake of my brother in Christ. 

There are times that I want to do anything that I don't consider sin like eating certain food, drinking alcohol or watching certain movies. However, more than my pleasure I should consider if people around me would be affected by my actions. Their souls and relationship with God should be in primary importance than my personal pleasure.
