Context of this verse is that there are some people in their church that does not obey what Paul teaches. He encourages the rest to persevere and do what is good. 

Don't go weary of doing good. Even if others do not. Even if it's hard. Even if it's painful. Even if it's against my comfort. Even if it's agains what I want. Do not go weary in doing the right things. It glorifies God in the first place, and secondly, it protects in integrity. Good counsel from Paul.
