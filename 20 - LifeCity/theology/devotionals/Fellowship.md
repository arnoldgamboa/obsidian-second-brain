Paul's charge to th the Corinthian church:
- Rejoice
- Aim for restoration (resolve conflicts)
- Comfort one another (help in times of need)
- Agree with one another (support each other)
- Live in peace (just get along well)
The result: God's live and peace is seen and experienced. 

As the leader of the church. I'm tasked to lead the way to seeing this happen. It's hard, though as I'm dealing with different personalities, attitudes and stages in spiritual life. Many times I feel like a failure whenever I don't see this happen. But as long as God is comanding me to lead, I will follow.
