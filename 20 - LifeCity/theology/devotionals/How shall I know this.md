The past weeks have been very discouraging. Relationship with K Prudy has gone sour and I know this has a great effect on how the church is run and the general atmosphere of the leadership is compromised. One of our leaders confessed of sexual sin. “Another bites the dust"

Our numbers aren’t improving. No new people are coming. Old people are dropping out. Indeed, there is reason to be discouraged.

God wills that his church to grow, to lead people to a relationship with his Son. To see lives changed.

But, “How shall I know this?”, when every indicator is pointing somewhere else? I’m doubting my calling as a leader of this church.

But should I doubt my calling as a follower of Jesus? By all means, with off of the remaining strength that I have, I should protect that. I am first and foremost a follower of Jesus and will remain so even if I’m not a leader of a church. Being a leader of the church should just be and will always be, simply an overflowing of my relationship with him. 

Lord, thank you for always reminding me of the most important things. Whenever I’m drifting away, you have a way to pull me back.
