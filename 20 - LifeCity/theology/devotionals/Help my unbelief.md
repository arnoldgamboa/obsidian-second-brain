“And it has often cast him into fire and into water, to destroy him. **But if you can do anything**, have compassion on us and help us.” And Jesus said to him, “‘**If you can’! All things are possible for one who believes.**” Immediately the father of the child cried out and said, **“I believe; help my unbelief!**””
‭‭Mark‬ ‭9:22-24‬ ‭ESV‬‬

Even if I know (head knowledge) that God can do anything, my human nature has the tendency to doubt and in fact not believe that he can. Just like this father in the story, I need to always declare that I believe and ask God to help me in my unbelief, and trust God with all the results. He will still heal, forgive, bless, and love -- he is not dependent on my capacity to believe. 

Thank you Lord for you can do what ever you wish not based on my capacity to believe. Right now, I need to be reminded of this fact. My faith in you has not wavered but because of the lack of results, I tend to not believe what my eyes cannot see. And yet I still pray that I believe! Help me see! Help my unbelief.
