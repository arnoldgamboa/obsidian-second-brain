Leper asked if Jesus wants him healed. It's not a matter of "can". It's a matter of "will". And Jesus in this case wants and he did. 

Sometimes there are obvious things that I know Jesus can do but not able to ask if he will. As part of my dynamic relationship with God, there has to be conversations like this. Honest questions, honest requests. God sometimes will say yes, sometimes no. But my task is to ask and in the process, my relationship with him grows.
