“All this is from God, who through Christ reconciled us to himself and gave us the ministry of reconciliation; that is, in Christ God was reconciling the world to himself, not counting their trespasses against them, and entrusting to us the message of reconciliation. Therefore, we are ambassadors for Christ, God making his appeal through us. We implore you on behalf of Christ, be reconciled to God.”
‭‭2 Corinthians‬ ‭5:18-20‬ ‭ESV‬‬
http://bible.com/59/2co.5.18-20.esv

Fine points:
- We're reconciled with God through Christ
- we are given the ministry of reconciliation 
- God is reconciling the world to himself
- and he is entrusting us this message of reconciliation 
- we therefore are ambassadors for Christ

Application? Whew! Such a priceless privilege to be entrusted with such important task. This verse has, supernaturally (since this is the message yesterday and this is my reading plan today), reinforced my longing to be an instrument of loving those who are far from God.
