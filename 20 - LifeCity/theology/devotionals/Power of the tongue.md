Very small yet powerful. Can be used to build up or tear down. Encourage or discourage. Brings life or death. That's the tongue. 

God has given me this power to use for the benefit of the people around me. Even of myself. When tempted to use my speech to tear apart, or discourage or pour out wrath though my words, my prayer is for the Holy Spirit to remind me of its proper use. May my speech be a tool to build up people, encourage and bring about life not just in the church that I care for but most especially in my family.
