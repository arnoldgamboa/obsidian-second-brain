“Count it all joy, my brothers, when you meet trials of various kinds, for you know that the testing of your faith produces steadfastness. And let steadfastness have its full effect, that you may be perfect and complete, lacking in nothing.”
‭‭James‬ ‭1:2-4‬ ‭ESV‬‬

James suggests what we normally shy away from: joy in the midst of trials. Reason? Because when faith is tested trough trials, it produces steadfastness, then faith is made complete because of this.

I also learned from going to the gym that resistance builds muscles. It is the "testing" of my body against weight that makes fats go away and muscles are produced instead. And I think spiritually speaking, trials bring my faith into test and grow it. Trials are also an opportunity for me to trust God to make me strong against it.
