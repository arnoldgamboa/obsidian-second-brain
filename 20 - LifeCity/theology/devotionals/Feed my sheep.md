Jesus is showing Peter the value and challenge of Pastoring. 

Feeding the sheep of God, while very rewarding, is also very challenging. The only way to do this is to be truly connected with the source -- Jesus. 

God is working in me right now on some changes that might challenge me not just as a leader but also as the one who takes care of the flock. I can only trust God that he knows what he's doing and that I'm making myself available as his servant.
