For they gave according to their means, as I can testify, and beyond their means, of their own accord, begging us earnestly for the favor of taking part in the relief of the saints— and this, not as we expected, but they gave themselves first to the Lord and then by the will of God to us.”

-Macedonean church in affliction, yet they are still generous.
- they gave according to their means
- not only that, they also gave BEYOND their means 
- this is a result of their relationship with God (v5)

I always thought that the generosity Paul teaches is "giving within your means". Here, he said that the Macedonian church gave even beyond their means. That's sacrificial giving. I'd love to have that attitude as an overflow of my relationship with God. Being generous is my most visible reflection of the character of a giving God.
