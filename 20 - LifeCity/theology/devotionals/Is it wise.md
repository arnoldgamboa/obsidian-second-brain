Question is not always "is it wrong?"  Sometimes the right question is "is it wise?"

Whenever I'm faced with tough questions in life, my default should not be to ask what is right or wrong. Most of the times, the Bible is clear in what is right and what is wrong -- I only need to obey. The question should be, in light of this situation, what's the wise thing to do? It's tougher and raises a higher standard. But it should be my life's guiding principle. 

Lord, please always guide me to asking the right questions so that I will not just obey you but also make wise decisions for myself, my family and people that look up to me.
