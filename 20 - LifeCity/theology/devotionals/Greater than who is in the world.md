"Little children, you are from God and have overcome them, for he who is in you is greater than he who is in the world. -1 John 4:4

Greater is Jesus who is in us — than anyone, including the enemy the devil, who is in the world. 

Sometimes, I’m overcome with guilt and frustrations of the my shortcomings. I feel defeated and overwhelmed. But today I’m reminded that Jesus, who is in me, is greater than all these challenges. I can overcome — I AM an overcomer! — because he who is in me is greater than anything and anyone  else.
