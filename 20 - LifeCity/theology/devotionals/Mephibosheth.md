Mephibosheth's story is a story of us. It's a story of grace. Just like Mephi, we're crippled by sin, and enemy of God because of our sinfulness. But only because of the King's grace and mercy can we sit at his table. The only difference now is that Jesus has to die just for that to happen. 

I'm humbled just by thinking about the kind of grace I've received from God. There is no thing on earth can prevent me from honoring my King.
