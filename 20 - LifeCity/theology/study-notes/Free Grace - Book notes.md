What is repentance?

“Metanoia” - change of mind

What needs to change?

1. Change your mind about Jesus 
	1. He’s not just a teacher
	2. He’s is God, Man, savior
1. Change your mind about your sins
	1. A change of mind **about your sin**. This involves realizing that sin is bad and that it offends a holy God who made you. You should hate sin, not love it
1. A change of mind **about yourself**. Instead of thinking you are a good person who is righteous and obedient enough, you should see yourself as a sinner in need of salvation
