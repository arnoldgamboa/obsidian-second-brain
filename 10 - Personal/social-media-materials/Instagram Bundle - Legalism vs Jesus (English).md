# Instagram Bundle: Legalism vs. Jesus (English)

**Post type:** Reels + Carousel crosspost
**Duration:** ~2 min (Reels) | 10 slides (Carousel)
**Language:** English
**Posting strategy:** Reels for reach, Carousel for saves/shares. Post together or 2-3 hours apart.

---

# PART 1 — REELS SCRIPT

## SLIDE 1 — COVER / HOOK

**On-screen text:**
Unlimited content. Missing the point.

**You say:**
I've noticed something on my feed lately.

---

## SLIDE 2 — THE OBSERVATION

**On-screen text:**
Same upbringing. Same group. So much content.

**You say:**
There's so much content right now from people who share the same religious upbringing as me. Same group I grew up in. And honestly? They have so much content.

---

## SLIDE 3 — THE CONTENT MACHINE

**On-screen text:**
Dress code. Saturday church. No pork. No seafood.

**You say:**
Because if you think about it, if your content is about the peculiar practices of your religion, you'll never run out. You can make content about how to dress properly, why Saturday is your church day instead of Sunday, no pork allowed, no seafood allowed — so much, right? Unlimited content.

---

## SLIDE 4 — THE QUESTION (MIC DROP)

**On-screen text:**
But where's the content about Jesus?

**You say:**
But here's my question: why does it seem like there's no content about who Jesus is? What the gospel is all about? The assurance of salvation that when you trust in Jesus alone, that's it — you're secured. And how that truth becomes your joy every single day.

None.

---

## SLIDE 5 — WHAT'S ACTUALLY THERE

**On-screen text:**
Dos and don'ts. True church. Different from others.

**You say:**
The usual topics: dos and don'ts. How to dress. What's forbidden. How they're different from other churches. How they're the "true" church. Just that.

---

## SLIDE 6 — THE DIAGNOSIS

**On-screen text:**
That's what legalism does.

**You say:**
So why is that the case? Because that's what legalism does to you. That's what happens when your religious organization is built on legalistic practices.

---

## SLIDE 7 — THE CONTRAST

**On-screen text:**
You focus on you. Not on Jesus.

**You say:**
You focus more on yourself. On your ability to do good. On your performance. Rather than on Jesus — the only One who is actually good.

---

## SLIDE 8 — THE HONESTY CHECK

**On-screen text:**
It's not just them. Sometimes it's us too.

**You say:**
And to be fair, it's not just them. Sometimes, even other Christians do this too. Sometimes we do too. We make our Christian walk about us — our works, our image, our standards.

---

## SLIDE 9 — THE MAIN THING

**On-screen text:**
The main thing is to keep the main thing the main thing.

**You say:**
But here's the thing: the main thing is to keep the main thing the main thing. And the main thing in your Christian walk is Jesus. Nothing else.

---

## SLIDE 10 — CTA / END CARD

**On-screen text:**
Jesus. Not rules.

**You say:**
*[Quiet nod, hold eye contact, cut]*

# PART 4 — POSTING STRATEGY

**Option A: Simultaneous post**
- Post Reels at 8 AM
- Post Carousel at 8 AM (same time, different format)
- Same caption on both
- Instagram algorithm sometimes suppresses simultaneous posts — test this

**Option B: Staggered post (recommended)**
- Post Carousel at 8 AM (better for morning scroll/saves)
- Post Reels at 11 AM or 2 PM (midday or afternoon break)
- Same caption, or Reels caption can be shorter with "Watch the full thought on Reels"

**Option C: Reels only, Carousel in Stories**
- Post Reels as main content
- Break Carousel into 10 Story slides with "Swipe up for more" or link sticker
- Good for engagement without splitting the algorithm

**Hashtag strategy:**
- Mix of large (#christianity #jesus) and niche (#christianliving #faithjourney)
- 15-20 hashtags total
- Place in caption (first comment also works but less effective now)

**Engagement CTA:**
- Carousel: "Save this" (encourages algorithm boost)
- Reels: "Share this with someone who needs to hear it"
- Both: "Comment AMEN if you agree" (only if authentic — don't force it)
