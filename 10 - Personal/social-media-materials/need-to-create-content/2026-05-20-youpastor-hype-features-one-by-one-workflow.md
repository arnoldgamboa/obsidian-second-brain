# YouPastor hype content workflow (feature-by-feature)

Mind dump:

Potential YouPastor hype posts/videos before beta should show features one by one as I actually use the product.

## Potential workflow

1. Pick one feature per post/video
   - Example: sermon brainstorm, passage research, church email draft, social repurpose, theological guardrails, approval flow.

2. Capture raw usage while building
   - 1 screenshot or 20–45s screen recording
   - real prompt/input
   - real output
   - quick note: what worked, what still needs fixing

3. Use repeatable post structure
   - Hook: real ministry pain
   - Demo: what YouPastor did
   - Reflection: what it solved / what it didn’t
   - CTA: join early list

4. Turn one demo into 3 derivatives
   - X: short opinion + one takeaway
   - Facebook: story-style build-in-public post
   - LinkedIn: practical operator angle + lesson

5. Keep momentum cadence
   - 2–3 feature posts per week until beta opens
   - 1 behind-the-scenes post per week (scar tissue / tradeoff)

6. Track with a simple content board
   - Backlog: features to show
   - Captured: recorded/screenshot ready
   - Drafted: copy written
   - Queued: scheduled in Buffer
   - Published: live with link to waitlist

7. Weekly review (user-centric)
   - Relevance: did pastors feel seen in their real weekly ministry pain?
   - Clarity + trust: did they understand YouPastor quickly and feel it supports, not replaces, pastoral judgment?
   - Action: did they take the next step (join list, ask for beta, reply), and what hesitations blocked them?
   - Feed those insights back into product priorities + next week content.

## Next action

Create a 10-feature pre-beta hype sequence and map each feature to one post + one optional short demo video.
