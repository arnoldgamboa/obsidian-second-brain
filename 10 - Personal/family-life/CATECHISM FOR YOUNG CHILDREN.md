<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">CATECHISM FOR YOUNG CHILDREN</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">An Introduction to the Shorter Catechism</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 1. Who made you?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. God.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 2. What else did God make?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. God made all things.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 3. Why did God make you and all things ?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. For his own glory.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 4. How can you glorify God?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. By loving him and doing what he commands.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 5. Why ought you to glorify God?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. Because he made me and takes care of me.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 6. Are there more gods than one?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. There is only one God.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 7. In how many persons does this one God exist?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. In three persons.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 8. What are they?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. The Father, the Son, and the Holy Ghost.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 9. What is God?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. God is a Spirit, and has not a body like men.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 10. Where is God?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. God is everywhere.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 11. Can you see God?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. No; I cannot see God, but he always sees me.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 12. Does God know all things?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. Yes; nothing can be hid from God.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 13. Can God do all things?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. Yes; God can do all his holy will.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 14. Where do you learn how to love and obey God?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. In the Bible alone.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 15. Who wrote the Bible?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. Holy men who were taught by the Holy Spirit.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 16. Who were our first parents?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. Adam and Eve.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 17. Of what were our first parents made?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. God made the body of Adam out of the ground, and formed Eve from the body of Adam.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 18. What did God give Adam and Eve besides bodies?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. He gave them souls that could never die.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 19. Have you a soul as well as a body?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. Yes; I have a soul that can never die.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 20. How do you know that you have a soul?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. Because the Bible tells me so.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 21. In what condition did God make Adam and Eve?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. He made them holy and happy.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 22. What is a covenant?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. An agreement between two or more persons.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 23. What covenant did God make with Adam?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. The covenant of works.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 24. What was Adam bound to do by the covenant of works?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. To obey God perfectly.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 25. What did God promise in the covenant of works?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. To reward Adam with life if he obeyed him.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 26. What did God threaten in the covenant of works?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. To punish Adam with death if he disobeyed.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 27. Did Adam keep the covenant of works?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. No; he sinned against God.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 28. What is Sin?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. Sin is any want of conformity unto, or transgression of the law of God.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 29. What is meant by want of conformity?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. Not being or doing what God requires.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 30. What is meant by transgression?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. Doing what God forbids.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 31. What was the sin of our first parents?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. Eating the forbidden fruit.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 32. Who tempted them to this sin?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. The devil tempted Eve, and she gave the fruit to Adam.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 33. What befell our first parents when they had sinned?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. Instead of being holy and happy, they became sinful and miserable.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 34. Did Adam act for himself alone in the covenant of works?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. No; he represented all his posterity.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 35. What effect had the sin of Adam on all mankind?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. All mankind are born in a state of sin and misery.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 36. What is that sinful nature which we inherit from Adam called?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. Original sin.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 37. What does every sin deserve?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. The wrath and curse of God.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 38. Can any one go to heaven with this sinful nature?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. No; our hearts must be changed before we can be fit for heaven.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 39. What is a change of heart called?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. Regeneration.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 40. Who can change a sinner’s heart?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. The Holy Spirit alone.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 41. Can any one be saved through the covenant of works?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. None can be saved through the covenant of works.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 42. Why can none be saved through the covenant of works?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. Because all have broken it, and are condemned by it</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 43. With whom did God the Father make the covenant of grace?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. With Christ, his eternal Son.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 44. Whom did Christ represent in the covenant of grace?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. His elect people.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 45. What did Christ undertake in the covenant of grace?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. To keep the whole law for his people, and to suffer the punishment due to their sins.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 46. Did our Lord Jesus Christ ever commit the least sin?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. No; he was holy, harmless, and undefiled.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 47. How could the Son of God suffer?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. Christ, the Son of God, became man that he might obey and suffer in our nature.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 48. What is meant by the Atonement?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. Christ’s satisfying divine justice, by his sufferings and death, in the place of sinners.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 49. What did God the Father undertake in the covenant of grace?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. To justify and sanctify those for whom Christ should die.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 50. What is justification?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. It is God’s forgiving sinners, and treating them as if they had never sinned.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 51. What is sanctification?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. It is God’s making sinners holy in heart and conduct.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 52. For whom did Christ obey and suffer?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. For those whom the Father had given him.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 53. What kind of life did Christ live on earth?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. A life of poverty and suffering.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 54. What kind of death did Christ die?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. The painful and shameful death of the cross.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 55. Who will be saved?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. Only those who repent of sin, believe in Christ, and lead holy lives.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 56. What is it to repent?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. To be sorry for sin, and to hate and forsake it because it is displeasing to God.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 57. What is it to believe or have faith in Christ?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. To trust in Christ alone for salvation.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 58. Can you repent and believe in Christ by your own power?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. No; I can do nothing good without the help of God’s Holy Spirit.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 59. How can you get the help of the Holy Spirit?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. God has told us that we must pray to him for the Holy Spirit.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 60. How long ago is it since Christ died?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. More than nineteen hundred years.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 61. How were pious persons saved before the coming of Christ?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. By believing in a Savior to come.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 62. How did they show their faith?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. By offering sacrifices on God’s altar.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 63. What did these sacrifices represent?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. Christ, the Lamb of God, who was to die for sinners.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 64. What offices has Christ?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. Christ has three offices.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 65. What are they?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. The offices of a prophet, of a priest, and of a king.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 66. How is Christ a prophet?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. Because he teaches us the will of God.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 67. How is Christ a priest?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. Because he died for our sins and pleads with God for us.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 68. How is Christ a king?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. Because he rules over us and defends us.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 69. Why do you need Christ as a prophet?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. Because I am ignorant.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 70. Why do you need Christ as a priest?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. Because I am guilty.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 71. Why do you need Christ as a king?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. Because I am weak and helpless.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 72. How many commandments did God give on Mount Sinai?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. Ten commandments.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 73. What are the ten commandments sometimes called?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. The Decalogue.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 74. What do the first four commandments teach?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. Our duty to God.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 75. What do the last six commandments teach?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. Our duty to our fellow men.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 76. What is the sum of the ten commandments?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. To love God with all my heart, and my neighbor as myself.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 77. Who is your neighbor?A. All my fellow men are my neighbors.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 78. Is God pleased with those who love and obey him?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. Yes; he says, “I love them that love me.”</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 79. Is God displeased with those who do not love and obey him?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. Yes; “God is angry with the wicked every day.”</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 80. What is the first commandment?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. The first commandment is, Thou shalt have no other gods before me.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 81. What does the first commandment teach us?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. To worship God alone.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 82. What is the second commandment?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. The second commandment is, Thou shalt not make unto thee any graven image, or any likeness of any things that is in heaven above, or that is in the earth beneath, or that is in the water under the earth; thou shalt not bow down thyself to them, nor serve them: for I, the Lord thy God, am a jealous God, visiting the iniquity of the fathers upon the children unto the third and fourth generation of them that hate me; and showing mercy unto thousands of them that love me, and keep my commandments.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 83. What does the second commandment teach us?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. To worship God in a proper manner, and to avoid idolatry.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 84. What is the third commandment?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. The third commandment is, Thou shalt not take the name of the Lord thy God in vain: for the Lord will not hold him guiltless that taketh his name in vain.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 85. What does the third commandment teach me?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. To reverence God’s name, word, and works.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 86. What is the fourth commandment?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. The fourth commandment is, Remember the Sabbath day to keep it holy. Six days shalt thou labor, and do all thy work, but the seventh day is the Sabbath of the Lord thy God; in it thou shalt not do any work, thou, nor thy son, nor thy daughter, nor thy manservant, nor thy maidservant, nor thy cattle, nor thy stranger that is within thy gates: for in six days the Lord made heaven and earth, the sea, and all that in them is, and rested the seventh day; wherefore the Lord blessed the Sabbath Day, and hallowed it.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 87. What does the fourth commandment teach us?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. To keep the Sabbath holy.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 88. What day of the week is the Christian Sabbath?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. The first day of the week, called the Lord’s day.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 89. Why is it called the Lord’s day?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. Because on that day Christ rose from the dead.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 90. How should the Sabbath be spent?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. In prayer and praise, in hearing and reading God’s Word, and in doing good to our fellow men.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 91. What is the fifth commandment?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. The fifth commandment is, Honor thy father and thy mother, that thy days may be long upon the land which the Lord thy God giveth thee.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 92. What does the fifth commandment teach me?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. To love and obey our parents and teachers.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 93. What is the sixth commandment?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. The sixth commandment is, Thou shalt not kill.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 94. What does the sixth commandment teach us?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. To avoid angry passions.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 95. What is the seventh commandment?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. The seventh commandment is, Thou shalt not commit adultery.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 96. What does the seventh commandment teach us?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. To be pure in heart, language, and conduct.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 97. What is the eighth commandment?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. The eighth commandment is, Thou shalt not steal.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 98. What does the eighth commandment teach us?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. To be honest and industrious.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 99. What is the ninth commandment?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. The ninth commandment is, Thou shalt not bear false witness against thy neighbor.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 100. What does the ninth commandment teach us?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. To tell the truth.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 101. What is the tenth commandment?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. The tenth commandment is, Thou shalt not covet thy neighbor’s house, thou shalt not covet thy neighbor’s wife, nor his manservant, nor his maidservant, nor his ox, nor his ass, nor any thing that is thy neighbor’s.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 102. What does the tenth commandment teach us?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. To be content with our lot.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 103. Can any man keep these ten commandments perfectly?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. No mere man, since the fall of Adam, ever did or can keep the ten commandments perfectly.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 104. Of what use are the ten commandments to us?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. They teach us our duty, and show our need of a Savior.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 105. What is prayer?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. Prayer is asking God for things which he has promised to give.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 106. In whose name should we pray?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. Only in the name of Christ.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 107. What has Christ given us to teach us how to pray?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. The Lord’s Prayer.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 108. Repeat the Lord’s Prayer.</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. Our Father which art in heaven, Hallowed be thy name. Thy kingdom come. Thy will be done in earth, as it is in heaven. Give us this day our daily bread. And forgive us our debts, as we forgive our debtors. And lead us not into temptation, but deliver us from evil: For thine is the kingdom, and the power, and the glory, for ever. Amen.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 109. How many petitions are there in The Lord’s Prayer?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. Six.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 110. What is the first petition?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. “Hallowed be thy name.”</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 111. What do we pray for in the first petition?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. That God’s name may be honored by us and all men.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 112. What is the second petition?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. “Thy kingdom come.”</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 113. What do we pray for in the second petition?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. That the gospel may be preached in all the world, and believed and obeyed by us and all men.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 114. What is the third petition?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. “Thy will be done in earth, as it is in heaven.”</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 115. What do we pray for in the third petition?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. That men on earth may serve God as the angels do in heaven.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 116. What is the fourth petition?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. “Give us this day our daily bread.”</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 117. What do we pray for in the fourth petition?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. That God would give us all things needful for our bodies and souls.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 118. What is the fifth petition?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. “And forgive us our debts, as we forgive our debtors.”</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 119. What do we pray for in the fifth petition?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. That God would pardon our sins for Christ’s sake, and enable us to forgive those who have injured us.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 120. What is the sixth petition?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. “And lead us not into temptation, but deliver us from evil.”</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 121. What do we pray for in the sixth petition?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. That God would keep us from sin.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 122. How many sacraments are there?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. Two.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 123. What are they?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. Baptism and the Lord’s Supper.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 124. Who appointed these sacraments?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. The Lord Jesus Christ.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 125. Why did Christ appoint these sacraments?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. To distinguish his disciples from the world, and to comfort and strengthen them.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 126. What sign is used in baptism?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. The washing with water.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 127. What does this signify?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. That we are cleansed from sin by the blood of Christ.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 128. In whose name are we baptized?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. In the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Ghost.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 129. Who are to be baptized?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. Believers</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 131. Does Christ care for little children?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. Yes; for he says, “Suffer the little children to come unto me, and forbid them not: for of such is the kingdom of God.”</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 132. To what does your baptism bind you?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. To be a true follower of Christ.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 133. What is the Lord’s Supper?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. The eating of bread and drinking of wine in remembrance of the sufferings and death of Christ.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 134. What does the bread represent?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. The body of Christ, broken for our sins.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 135. What does the wine represent?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. The blood of Christ, shed for our salvation.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 136. Who should partake of the Lord’s Supper?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. Only those who repent of their sins, believe in Christ for salvation, and love their fellow men.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 137. Did Christ remain in the tomb after his crucifixion?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. No; he rose from the tomb on the third day after his death.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 138. Where is Christ now?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. In heaven, interceding for sinners.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 139. Will he come again?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. Yes; at the last day Christ will come to judge the world.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 140. What becomes of men at death?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. The body returns to dust, and the soul goes into the world of spirits.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 141. Will the bodies of the dead be raised to life again?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. Yes; “The trumpet shall sound, and the dead shall be raised.”</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 142. What will become of the wicked in the day of judgment?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. They shall be cast into hell.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 143. What is hell?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. A place of dreadful and endless torment.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 144. What will become of the righteous?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. They shall be taken to heaven.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">Q. 145. What is heaven?</span>
<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;color:#666666ff;">A. A glorious and happy place, where the righteous shall be forever with the Lord.</span>
