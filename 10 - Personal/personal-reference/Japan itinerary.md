Day 1 (Monday):
- Arrive at Narita Airport at 2 pm
- Transfer to your hotel in Tokyo (consider staying in Shinjuku, Shibuya, or Ginza for convenient access to major attractions)
- Explore the neighborhood around your hotel, and try local Japanese cuisine for dinner
Day 2 (Tuesday):
- Morning: Visit Asakusa, explore Senso-ji Temple and Nakamise Street
- Afternoon: Head to Akihabara, Tokyo's electronics and anime district
- Evening: Visit Odaiba, an artificial island with shopping, dining, and entertainment options. See the giant Gundam statue, and enjoy the nighttime view of Rainbow Bridge
Day 3 (Wednesday):
- Full day at Tokyo Disneyland or Tokyo DisneySea (choose one based on your preference). Make sure to arrive early to maximize your time and minimize waiting in lines
Day 4 (Thursday):
- Morning: Visit Tsukiji Fish Market for a sushi breakfast
- Afternoon: Explore Ginza, a high-end shopping district, and visit the Kabukiza Theatre to catch a kabuki performance
- Evening: Visit Roppongi Hills for a panoramic view of Tokyo from the Mori Tower observation deck
Day 5 (Friday):
- Morning: Head to Lake Kawaguchiko for a day trip to Mt. Fuji. Enjoy the view of the mountain from the lake, and explore nearby attractions like the Oishi Park and Kawaguchiko Music Forest
- Evening: Return to Tokyo, and explore the nightlife in Shibuya or Shinjuku
Day 6 (Saturday):
- Morning: Visit Meiji Shrine, a Shinto shrine dedicated to Emperor Meiji and Empress Shoken
- Afternoon: Head to Harajuku and Omotesando for shopping and street fashion
- Evening: Visit the Hana Biyori Greenhouse at Yomiuriland Amusement Park, and enjoy the night illuminations
Day 7 (Sunday):
- Morning: Visit Ueno Park, and explore the Tokyo National Museum, the National Museum of Nature and Science, or the Ueno Zoo
- Afternoon: Stroll through Yanaka, a historic neighborhood with traditional Japanese architecture
- Evening: Enjoy a leisurely dinner in a traditional izakaya or try kaiseki, a multi-course Japanese meal
Day 8 (Monday):
- Morning: Visit Odaiba's TeamLab Borderless, a digital art museum
- Afternoon: Do some last-minute shopping in Ginza or Shibuya
- Evening: Transfer to Narita Airport, and depart at 9 pm
This itinerary covers a mix of iconic sights, cultural experiences, and leisure time in Tokyo, with visits to Tokyo Disney, Mt. Fuji, and Hana Biyori Greenhouse. Adjust the plan according to your interests and pace.

Version 2—

**Day 1: July 10, Monday - Arrival in Tokyo**
- Arrive at Narita or Haneda International Airport.
- Transfer to your hotel in Asakusa.
- Explore local area: Senso-ji temple and Nakamise Shopping Street, if energy allows.
**Day 2: July 11, Tuesday - Tokyo Disneyland**
- Spend the whole day at Tokyo Disneyland.
**Day 3: July 12, Wednesday - Explore Asakusa and Ueno**
- Morning: Visit Tokyo Skytree and Sumida Aquarium.
- Afternoon: Head to Ueno. Visit Ueno Zoo and the museums in Ueno Park, such as Tokyo National Museum and National Museum of Nature and Science.
- Evening: Explore Ameya-Yokocho Market.
**Day 4: July 13, Thursday - Shinjuku, Harajuku, and Shibuya**
- Morning: Visit Shinjuku Gyoen National Garden in Shinjuku.
- Midday: Head to Harajuku. Explore Takeshita Street and Yoyogi Park.
- Afternoon: Head to Shibuya. Witness the famous Shibuya Crossing and visit Hachiko statue.
- Evening: Head back to Shinjuku. Visit Tokyo Metropolitan Government Building for a panoramic view of Tokyo, and explore the lively nightlife of Shinjuku.
**Day 5: July 14, Friday - Akihabara and Ginza**
- Morning: Explore Akihabara.
- Afternoon: Head to Ginza for shopping and a sushi lunch at Tsukiji Fish Market.
- Evening: Visit Roppongi Hills or Tokyo Midtown in the Roppongi district for fine dining and a cityscape view.
**Day 6: July 15, Saturday - Day trip to Mount Fuji**
- Full day trip to Mount Fuji including a visit to Lake Kawaguchi and Oshino Hakkai village.
- Return to Tokyo in the evening.
**Day 7: July 16, Sunday - Yomiuriland Amusement Park and Odaiba**
- Morning and early afternoon: Visit Yomiuriland Amusement Park. Don't forget to explore the Hana Biyori Greenhouse.
- Late afternoon and Evening: Head to Odaiba. Visit Palette Town, Odaiba Seaside Park, and enjoy the view of Tokyo's skyline from the Rainbow Bridge.
- Dinner at Aqua City or Decks.
**Day 8: July 17, Monday - Departure Day**
- Depending on your flight time, you might be able to squeeze in some last-minute shopping or sightseeing.
- Transfer to Narita or Haneda International Airport.
- Say goodbye to Tokyo and have a safe journey back home!
Again, remember to check the opening times for all attractions as they can vary and may be different due to public holidays or special events. Enjoy your trip!
