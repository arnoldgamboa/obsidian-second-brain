# Instagram Video Script: Legalism vs. Jesus

**Platform:** Instagram Reels / TikTok
**Duration:** ~2 minutes
**Language:** Taglish
**Tone:** Conversational, reflective, engaging

---

## SCRIPT

**[HOOK — 0:00–0:05]**

**[Arnold on cam, casual, slight pause before speaking]**

May napansin ako sa feed ko lately.

**[0:05–0:20]**

Ang daming content ngayon from people na same religious upbringing ko. You know, yung same group na lumaki ako. And honestly? Ang dami nilang content.

**[0:20–0:40]**

Kasi if you think about it, if ang topic mo ay yung mga peculiar practices ng religion mo, you'll never run out of content. Pwede kang mag-content about how to dress properly, bakit Saturday ang church day mo instead of Sunday, bawal kumain ng pork, bawal ang seafood — ang dami, diba? Unlimited content.

**[0:40–1:05]**

Pero eto yung tanong ko: bakit parang wala akong nakikitang content about who Jesus is? What the gospel is all about? Yung assurance of salvation na kapag nagtiwala ka kay Jesus alone, that's it — secured ka na. And how that truth becomes your joy every single day.

Wala.

**[1:05–1:25]**

Ang laging topic: dos and don'ts. Paano ka mag-dress. Ano ang bawal. How they're different from other churches. How they're the "true" church. Puro gano'n.

**[1:25–1:45]**

So why is that the case? Kasi that's what legalism does to you. That's what happens when your religious organization is built on legalistic practices. You focus more on yourself. On your ability to do good. On your performance. Rather than on Jesus — the only One who is actually good.

**[1:45–1:55]**

And to be fair, hindi lang sila. Minsan, even other Christians do this too. Minsan tayo rin. We make our Christian walk about us — our works, our image, our standards.

**[1:55–2:05]**

Pero here's the thing: the main thing is to keep the main thing the main thing. And the main thing in your Christian walk is Jesus. Nothing else.

**[2:05–2:10]**

**[Hold on last line, soft nod, cut]**

---

## OPTIONAL CAPTION

Kung ang focus ng Christianity mo ay yung mga rules mo, baka hindi si Jesus ang center — baka ikaw.

## NOTES
- Speak slowly on the hook. Let the pause land.
- The word "wala" at 1:05 should feel like a mic drop moment.
- The line "rather than on Jesus" is the emotional peak — lean into it.
- End with quiet confidence, not preachy energy.
