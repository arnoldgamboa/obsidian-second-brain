# Instagram Carousel: Legalism vs. Jesus (English Version)

**Format:** Instagram Carousel (10 slides)
**Tone:** Conversational, reflective, direct
**Design notes:** Black text on off-white or muted background. One idea per slide.

---

## SLIDE 1 — COVER

**Headline:**
Unlimited content. But missing the point.

**Subhead:**
Something I noticed about Christian content lately.

**[Design note: Bold, centered. Minimal. Swipe arrow.]**

---

## SLIDE 2 — THE OBSERVATION

**Headline:**
Lately, my feed is full of it.

**Body:**
From people who share the same religious upbringing as me. Same group. Same practices.

And honestly? They have so much content.

---

## SLIDE 3 — THE CONTENT MACHINE

**Headline:**
If your content is about religious rules, you'll never run out.

**Body:**
• How to dress properly
• Why Saturday, not Sunday
• No pork allowed
• No seafood allowed

Unlimited content. But...

---

## SLIDE 4 — THE QUESTION

**Headline:**
But where's the content about Jesus?

**Body:**
Where's the content about:

• Who Jesus is?
• What the gospel is all about?
• The assurance of salvation?
• How that truth becomes your joy every single day?

**None.**

---

## SLIDE 5 — WHAT'S ACTUALLY THERE

**Headline:**
What they actually talk about:

**Body:**
Dos and don'ts.

How to dress.
What's forbidden.
How they're different from other churches.
How they're the "true" church.

Just that.

---

## SLIDE 6 — THE DIAGNOSIS

**Headline:**
Why is that the case?

**Body:**
Because that's what legalism does.

When your religious organization is built on legalistic practices, you focus more on yourself. On your ability to do good. On your performance.

---

## SLIDE 7 — THE CONTRAST

**Headline:**
Rather than on Jesus.

**Body:**
The only One who is actually good.

The only One who can save.
The only One worth centering your entire life on.

---

## SLIDE 8 — THE HONESTY CHECK

**Headline:**
And to be fair, it's not just them.

**Body:**
Sometimes, even other Christians do this too.
Sometimes, we do too.

We make our Christian walk about us — our works, our image, our standards.

---

## SLIDE 9 — THE MAIN THING

**Headline:**
The main thing is to keep the main thing the main thing.

**Body:**
And the main thing in your Christian walk is Jesus.

Nothing else.

---

## SLIDE 10 — CTA / END CARD

**Headline:**
Jesus. Not rules.

**Body:**
If your faith feels heavy, maybe the center of it is your rules — not Jesus.

The gospel is not about what you must do.
It's about what Jesus already did.
