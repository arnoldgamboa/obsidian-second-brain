# Instagram Bundle: Legalism vs. Jesus

**Post type:** Reels + Carousel crosspost
**Duration:** ~2 min (Reels) | 10 slides (Carousel)
**Language:** Taglish
**Posting strategy:** Reels for reach, Carousel for saves/shares. Post together or 2-3 hours apart.

---

# PART 1 — REELS SCRIPT

## SLIDE 1 — COVER / HOOK

**On-screen text:**
Unlimited content. Missing the point.

**You say:**
May napansin ako sa feed ko lately.

---

## SLIDE 2 — THE OBSERVATION

**On-screen text:**
Same upbringing. Same group. So much content.

**You say:**
Ang daming content ngayon from people na same religious upbringing ko. Same group na lumaki ako. And honestly? Ang dami nilang content.

---

## SLIDE 3 — THE CONTENT MACHINE

**On-screen text:**
Dress code. Saturday church. No pork. No seafood.

**You say:**
Kasi if you think about it, if ang topic mo ay yung mga peculiar practices ng religion mo, you'll never run out of content. Pwede kang mag-content about how to dress properly, bakit Saturday ang church day mo instead of Sunday, bawal kumain ng pork, bawal ang seafood — ang dami, diba? Unlimited content.

---

## SLIDE 4 — THE QUESTION (MIC DROP)

**On-screen text:**
Pero nasaan yung tungkol kay Jesus?

**You say:**
Pero eto yung tanong ko: bakit parang wala akong nakikitang content about who Jesus is? What the gospel is all about? Yung assurance of salvation na kapag nagtiwala ka kay Jesus alone, that's it — secured ka na. And how that truth becomes your joy every single day.

Wala.

---

## SLIDE 5 — WHAT'S ACTUALLY THERE

**On-screen text:**
Dos and don'ts. True church. Different from others.

**You say:**
Ang laging topic: dos and don'ts. Paano ka mag-dress. Ano ang bawal. How they're different from other churches. How they're the "true" church. Puro gano'n.

---

## SLIDE 6 — THE DIAGNOSIS

**On-screen text:**
That's what legalism does.

**You say:**
So why is that the case? Kasi that's what legalism does to you. That's what happens when your religious organization is built on legalistic practices.

---

## SLIDE 7 — THE CONTRAST

**On-screen text:**
You focus on you. Not on Jesus.

**You say:**
You focus more on yourself. On your ability to do good. On your performance. Rather than on Jesus — the only One who is actually good.

---

## SLIDE 8 — THE HONESTY CHECK

**On-screen text:**
Hindi lang sila. Minsan tayo rin.

**You say:**
And to be fair, hindi lang sila. Minsan, even other Christians do this too. Minsan tayo rin. We make our Christian walk about us — our works, our image, our standards.

---

## SLIDE 9 — THE MAIN THING

**On-screen text:**
The main thing is to keep the main thing the main thing.

**You say:**
Pero here's the thing: the main thing is to keep the main thing the main thing. And the main thing in your Christian walk is Jesus. Nothing else.

---

## SLIDE 10 — CTA / END CARD

**On-screen text:**
Jesus. Not rules.

**You say:**
*[Quiet nod, hold eye contact, cut]*

# PART 3 — CROSSPOST CAPTION

This caption works for either the Reels or the Carousel. If posting both, use the same caption or slightly tweak the CTA.

---

**Caption:**

Unlimited content about rules. But where's Jesus?

Napansin ko lang sa feed ko lately. Ang daming Christian content from people na same upbringing ko — about dress code, Sabbath day, bawal na pagkain, how their church is the "true" one.

Valid naman yung mga topics na yun. Pero parang may missing.

Nasaan yung content about who Jesus is?
About the gospel?
About the assurance na kapag nagtiwala ka kay Jesus, secured ka na?
About how that truth becomes your joy?

Wala.

That's what legalism does. It makes you focus on yourself — your performance, your rules, your standards. Rather than on Jesus, the only One who is actually good.

And to be fair, hindi lang sila. Minsan tayo rin.

The main thing is to keep the main thing the main thing.
And the main thing in your Christian walk is Jesus.

Nothing else.

# PART 4 — POSTING STRATEGY

**Option A: Simultaneous post**
- Post Reels at 8 AM
- Post Carousel at 8 AM (same time, different format)
- Same caption on both
- Instagram algorithm sometimes suppresses simultaneous posts — test this

**Option B: Staggered post (recommended)**
- Post Carousel at 8 AM (better for morning scroll/saves)
- Post Reels at 11 AM or 2 PM (midday or afternoon break)
- Same caption, or Reels caption can be shorter with "Watch the full thought on Reels"

**Option C: Reels only, Carousel in Stories**
- Post Reels as main content
- Break Carousel into 10 Story slides with "Swipe up for more" or link sticker
- Good for engagement without splitting the algorithm

**Hashtag strategy:**
- Mix of large (#christianity #jesus) and niche (#pinoychristian #taglish)
- 15-20 hashtags total
- Place in caption (first comment also works but less effective now)

**Engagement CTA:**
- Carousel: "Save this" (encourages algorithm boost)
- Reels: "Share this with someone who needs to hear it"
- Both: "Comment AMEN if you agree" (only if authentic — don't force it)
