## Workout A: Push Focus
Warm-up (5 minutes)
	•	2 min brisk walk/light cardio
	•	Arm circles, shoulder rolls
	•	10 bodyweight squats
	•	10 push-ups (modified if needed)
Main Workout (35 minutes)
	1.	Barbell/Dumbbell Bench Press
	•	3 sets x 8-10 reps
	•	Rest: 90 seconds
	2.	Goblet Squats
	•	3 sets x 10-12 reps
	•	Rest: 60 seconds
	3.	Dumbbell Shoulder Press
	•	3 sets x 8-10 reps
	•	Rest: 60 seconds
	4.	Dumbbell Romanian Deadlift
	•	3 sets x 10-12 reps
	•	Rest: 60 seconds
	5.	Push-ups or Chest Fly
	•	2 sets x 12-15 reps
	•	Rest: 45 seconds
	6.	Plank Hold
	•	3 sets x 30-45 seconds
	•	Rest: 45 seconds
Cool-down (5 minutes)
	•	Light stretching focusing on chest, shoulders, legs
	•	Deep breathing exercises

## Workout B: Pull Focus
Warm-up (5 minutes)
	•	2 min light cardio
	•	Arm swings, leg swings
	•	Cat-cow stretches
	•	10 bodyweight lunges
Main Workout (35 minutes)
	1.	Dumbbell/Barbell Row
	•	3 sets x 8-10 reps
	•	Rest: 90 seconds
	2.	Leg Press or Front Squats
	•	3 sets x 10-12 reps
	•	Rest: 60 seconds
	3.	Lat Pulldown or Assisted Pull-ups
	•	3 sets x 8-10 reps
	•	Rest: 60 seconds
	4.	Dumbbell Step-ups
	•	3 sets x 10 reps per leg
	•	Rest: 60 seconds
	5.	Bicep Curls
	•	2 sets x 12-15 reps
	•	Rest: 45 seconds
	6.	Side Plank
	•	2 sets x 20-30 seconds per side
	•	Rest: 45 seconds
Cool-down (5 minutes)
	•	Stretching focusing on back, biceps, legs
	•	Gentle spine mobility
## Workout C: Full Body Power
Warm-up (5 minutes)
	•	2 min light cardio
	•	Dynamic stretches
	•	Hip circles
	•	10 wall push-ups
Main Workout (35 minutes)
	1.	Trap Bar Deadlift (or DB Deadlift)
	•	3 sets x 6-8 reps
	•	Rest: 2 minutes
	2.	Incline Dumbbell Press
	•	3 sets x 8-10 reps
	•	Rest: 60 seconds
	3.	Bulgarian Split Squats
	•	3 sets x 10 reps per leg
	•	Rest: 60 seconds
	4.	Cable/Band Face Pulls
	•	3 sets x 12-15 reps
	•	Rest: 45 seconds
	5.	Dumbbell Hammer Curls
	•	2 sets x 12 reps
	•	Rest: 45 seconds
	6.	Hanging Knee Raises or Reverse Crunches
	•	3 sets x 10-12 reps
	•	Rest: 45 seconds
Cool-down (5 minutes)
	•	Full body stretching
	•	Focus on hip flexors and lower back
