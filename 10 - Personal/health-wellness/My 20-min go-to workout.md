<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">You may notice that there’s a lot of fitness/workout apps that’s being advertised on your feed nowadays. Clever app developers!  — of course, it’s new year, to get fit is your number 1 new year’s resolution.</span>

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">In my opinion, though, you don’t need an app. You need commitment, develop a habit and be consistent. 80% of those who started going to the gym on January 1 drops off before the end of the month. What you need is just a simple routine to keep you get started and be committed to it while developing a habit. I can tell you by experience, commitment and consistency is the hardest!</span>

 <span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">So, I’m sharing my go to workout routine when I’m busy or didn’t have my workout partner with me. This HIIT (high-intensity interval training) workout only needs  20 mins to accomplish and very easy and simple (beginners to intermediate). This only needs dumbbells, designed to make your whole body move and yes, lose weight!</span> 

<span style="font-size:11.142857551574707pt;">Rules: Do this for 4 to 6 sets (I normally do 5 sets. In my mind, I thought I didn’t do less while cheating of not doing the full 6 sets. 😄 ) Between exercises, rest for 30 secs. Don’t go over that, you need your heart pumping to make this work. In between sets, rest for 1 min.</span>
