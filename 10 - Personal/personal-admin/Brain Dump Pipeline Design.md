# Brain Dump Pipeline Design

Brain dump — no immediate action needed.

## Problem

How to make brain dumps useful long-term instead of letting them die in a folder.

## Proposed Lifecycle

Dump → Process → Distill → Activate

1. **Dump** — Raw capture into `brain/+`
2. **Process** — Weekly review: categorize by PARA
3. **Distill** — Extract patterns, decisions, content ideas
4. **Activate** — Turn into tasks, blog posts, systems, or trash

## Long-Game Value

Over 6–12 months, the archive becomes:
- Decision journal — "What was I thinking when I chose this tech stack?"
- Content mine — blog posts, threads, product ideas
- Automation roadmap — every repeated frustration becomes a system
- Personal knowledge graph — connected notes in Obsidian that compound
