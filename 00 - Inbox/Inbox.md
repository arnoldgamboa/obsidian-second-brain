# Inbox (+)

Landing zone for all uncategorized notes, braindumps, and quick captures.

## How to Use

1. **Dump freely** — Drop anything here. Voice memos, screenshots, links, half-thoughts, meeting notes, ideas.
2. **Tag me** — Message me on Telegram with `process inbox` or just dump directly and I'll review.
3. **Weekly review** — Every Friday, I'll generate a digest and suggest actions.

## What Happens During Processing

For each item in the inbox, I will:

| Action | Result |
|--------|--------|
| **File** | Move to Projects, Areas, Resources, or Archives |
| **Task** | Add to Todoist with context |
| **Content** | Draft a blog post, thread, or sermon idea |
| **Trash** | Delete noise |
| **Connect** | Link related notes in Obsidian |

## Weekly Digest Format

```
INBOX DIGEST — Week of [date]

New Dumps: [N] items
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
[1] "Note title" → Suggested action
[2] "Note title" → Suggested action
...

Actions Taken:
- Filed X items to [location]
- Created Y Todoist tasks
- Drafted Z content pieces
- Deleted W noise items

Patterns & Insights:
- Recurring theme: ...
- Content opportunity: ...
- Decision to log: ...
```

## Long-Game Value

Over 6–12 months, your brain dump archive becomes:
- **Decision journal** — "What was I thinking when I chose this tech stack?"
- **Content mine** — Blog posts, threads, product ideas
- **Automation roadmap** — Every repeated frustration becomes a system
- **Personal knowledge graph** — Connected notes in Obsidian that compound
