## How mind dumps work

1. New undeveloped thoughts are captured here with `status: inbox` and the `mind-dump` tag.
2. During processing, each note moves into its actual domain folder.
3. The `mind-dump` tag remains as provenance, while `status` changes to `filed`.
4. Tasks are suggested separately and are not automatically added to Todoist.
