One indicator that Leni’s campaign is coming from the grassroots level is the very apparent lack of singular messeging and branding. Hashtags are all over the place. Do we focus on her capabilities, incorruptibility or accomplishments? There’s no one strong message to campaign for. And, the brand itself — yes it’s pink, but what type of pink?  What’s the official font type? Do we say, “Data is Leni”, “Let Leni Lead”, or “LabanLeni” in our signage? It’s chaotic — beautifully chaotic. 

It’s positive and negative at the same time. Positive for obvious reasons that when campaign starts with the people, nobody, no Billions of pesos can top that. It becomes negative if the official campaign becomes complacent and will not come up with a singular message that sticks. There still needs to have that message that will unite all of its supporters around. It’s too early, campaign has not even started yet — I’m sure the official Leni campaign team is working on that. In the meantime, let’s enjoy the chaos.
