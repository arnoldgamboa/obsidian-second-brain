Months before the start of the campaign period, I was openly saying that the Filipinos are NOT worth dying for. That I lost hope in the Filipino. We can’t be trusted in electing good public servants. We decide on trivialities — you know, he can cuss, “he must be ‘masa’”.  We elect based on family name even if they’re corrupt. Why would you die for the Filipino?

The immaturity of our electorate was too much to bear I promised not to participate in the electoral process anymore.

Until this Leni campaign. I saw the passion. The volunteerism. And — I thought — maybe the people will see the obvious this time around. The Marcoses, they’ve rapped the nation. They robbed our coffers. They enjoyed lavish living at our expense. Can’t miss it, it’s clear as day light.

Is it?

No. Robin Padilla is the evidence.

Robin Padilla. No, really? Robin Padilla is elected as the no. 1 senator of the Republic of the Philippines?!

Not even Tulfo, who at least did some TV public service with his messed up Tulfo-brand of justice.

Not even Alan Peter Cayetano?

Robin Padilla over public attorney Chel Diokno?

Robin Padilla over international law expert (daw) spox Harry Roque?

No. We elected as no. 1 senator Robin ‘effing Padilla! Anak ni Baby Ama.

Do we even know what a senator does? Do we think we’re electing best actors for FAMAS?

Where has the Jovito Salongas and the Raul Rocos and Rene Saguisags gone? 

Robin Padilla.

No wonder we’re ok electing magnanakaw, sinungaling — because, unity. More than half of us are ok with that. That’s how f*cked up our electorate is. 

Balakayojan.

Arnold Gamboa
May 10, 2022
Signing off.
