Self-hosting is the practice of hosting and managing applications on your own server(s) instead of consuming from <a href="https://www.gnu.org/philosophy/who-does-that-server-really-serve.html" rel="noopener" class="external-link" target="_blank"><u>SaaSS</u></a> providers.
