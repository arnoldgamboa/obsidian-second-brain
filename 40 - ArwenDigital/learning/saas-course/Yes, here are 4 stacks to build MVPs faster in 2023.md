Whether you are a full-time Indie Hacker, a Freelancer or just trying to make a side project, **you need to ship and launch your product faster**.
I’ve been building personal projects since 2011 and, the more I tried to do things in a complicated way, the less I shipped and launched my projects.
The key to getting shit done is to build an MVP (minimum viable product) or an MLP (minimum lovable product), and build it fast.
