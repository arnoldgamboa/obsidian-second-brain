## Who we are:
Since 2011, we’ve been on a mission to analyze and summarize the latest scientific research to help you be healthier. And it’s a [mission](https://examine.com/about/) we take very seriously! We stay laser-focused on improving people’s lives, being transparent, and balancing free vs. paid content. Examine is wholly independent and prides itself in having no external sources of funding. Our guiding principles are accuracy and objectivity. And with the successful launch of Examine 2.0 recently completed, we’re excited to expand in multiple areas, including a YouTube channel and podcast, advertising, marketing, business development, and more.

## What the job is:
Examine is expanding its development team and needs someone to guide the team while keeping things organized and on track.

We’re looking for a curious, open-minded, and tech-savvy individual with experience managing a development team and understanding the big picture and the nitty-gritty of how things operate.
This job will involve you in all facets of the company.
**Responsibilities**:
- Lead the development team.
- Assign tasks to team members and ensure that all deliverables are completed on schedule.
- Build processes, workflows, and standard operating procedures for the team.
- Minimize administrative work for team members to ensure focus on coding.
- Onboard new team members and provide training as required.
- Monitor team performance, identify areas of improvement, and provide feedback to team members.
- Ensure that project documentation is accurate, up-to-date, and accessible to the team.
- Monitor and communicate project progress, identify risks, mitigate risks, and resolve issues.
- Primary liaison between the dev team and rest of the organization
- Identifying roles that may need hiring.

**Skills and Requirements:**
- Experience leading a team: managing, deliverables, organizing processes, and more.
- Experience with PHP.
- Familiarity with Laravel and javascript is a plus.
- Familiarity with Examine is a plus.

Having experience submitting to open-source software is a plus.
To repeat: It’s of utmost importance that you’re open-minded, curious, and easy to work with.
