I am writing a book with the idea and theological basis of Free Grace ( I obviously should not tell that it’s based on that, but the discussion will be based on that theological position)  . It  should cover the following:
- The goal of the book is to present that salvation is by grace alone through faith alone. Not by doing good works. You simply believe that Jesus is savior and king.
- It should expound what belief meant and how salvation is based on that belief alone. 
- The idea is to present a non-combative approach in presenting this idea.
- It should recognize that this is just one of the ideas about salvation.
- This book will not try to say that the reader’s current belief system is wrong.
- However, what the book will try to prove is that the different current religious system does say that to be saved means to trust in Jesus + some form of doing good or good works.
- I will then present faithfully the belief system of each of the following
	- Roman Catholism
	- Seventh-day Adventism
	- Iglesia ni Cristo
	- Mormonism
	- Jehova’s Witnesses
	- Islam
- In presenting these, I will  each presentation by showing how the belief system  is salvation = faith + works.
- After faithfully presenting these beliefs, I will try to bring the reading into trying to consider the beauty of believing that salvation is only by grace and not with works
- It might bring them some understanding of the beauty of this belief if I show the benefits of this
- Show them the benefits of salvation by grace through faith alone
	- In our understanding of who god is
	- In our daily lives
	- In how we see the future
	- All of these will give them the peace, joy and love they need to live by. 
- Draw some conclusions without pushing the idea. Give the reader the space to think and consider without saying that the book’s presentation is what they correct and theirs is wrong
- The whole idea behind this book is to present salvation by grace through faith alone without works so compelling they will consider it over their current belief system.
- The question that I would be asking at the end would be something like “Why is your belief more compelling than this?”
