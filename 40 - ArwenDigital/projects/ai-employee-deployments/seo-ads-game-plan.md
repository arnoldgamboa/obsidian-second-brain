# SEO + Paid Ads Game Plan

**Goal:** Generate 5–10 qualified 15-min consultation calls per week within 60 days.  
**Primary channel mix:** SEO (long-term) + LinkedIn Ads (B2B targeting) + organic content (credibility).  
**Secondary:** Facebook Ads (broader reach, retargeting).

---

## Phase 1: Foundation (Week 1–2)

Before you spend a dollar on ads, lock these down:

### 1. Landing Page Live
- Host on your existing personal site or a fast single-page builder (Carrd / Webflow / Framer).
- Page must load < 2 seconds. Mobile-first design.
- Cal.com embed above the fold AND at the bottom.
- Install Meta Pixel + LinkedIn Insight Tag for retargeting.

### 2. Conversion Tracking
- **Cal.com:** Set up Google Analytics 4 event tracking for "booking_completed."
- **Meta Pixel:** Create Custom Conversion for Cal.com thank-you page.
- **LinkedIn:** Install Insight Tag + set up conversion tracking for thank-you page.
- **UTM discipline:** Every ad, every post, every link gets a UTM. Example:
  `https://cal.com/arnoldgamboa/15min?utm_source=linkedin&utm_medium=paid&utm_campaign=ai-agent-law-firms`

### 3. Baseline Content
Publish these 3 pieces BEFORE running paid traffic:
1. "How We Built a Digital Employee for a [Vertical] in 48 Hours" (case study format — even if hypothetical, write the playbook).
2. "OpenClaw vs. Hermes: Which Agent Stack Should Your Business Use?" (educational, ranks for branded terms).
3. "The $5K AI Agent: What You Actually Get (No Fluff)" (transparent pricing post — high intent).

## Phase 3: LinkedIn Ads (Primary Paid Channel)

LinkedIn is your #1 paid channel because:
- You target by job title (Partner, CEO, Managing Director, Agency Owner).
- You target by industry (Legal, Marketing, Real Estate, Insurance).
- B2B decision makers live here.

### Campaign Structure

**Campaign 1: Awareness → Retargeting**
- **Objective:** Website visits.
- **Audience:** Job titles = CEO, Founder, Managing Partner, Agency Owner. Industries = Legal, Marketing, Real Estate, Insurance, Manufacturing.
- **Company size:** 1–50 employees (your sweet spot — big enough to afford $5K, small enough to feel the pain).
- **Geo:** Start with Philippines + English-speaking markets if you can serve remotely. If US-only, target US.
- **Ad format:** Single image + short copy. Link to blog post #1 or #3.
- **Budget:** $20–30/day.

**Campaign 2: Conversion — Direct CTA**
- **Objective:** Website conversions (Cal.com booking).
- **Audience:** Retargeting — anyone who visited the landing page or blog in the last 30 days.
- **Ad format:** Sponsored Content with a strong CTA.
- **Budget:** $30–50/day.

### LinkedIn Ad Copy Templates

**Ad A: The Provocation**
> Most businesses don’t need another AI tool. They need someone to run it for them.
>
> We install and manage AI agents as digital employees — one flat fee, zero learning curve.
>
> First agent live in 48 hours.
>
> 👇 Book a free 15-min call.

**Ad B: The Vertical Hook (Law Firm Example)**
> Law firm partners are losing 15+ hours a week to intake, follow-ups, and scheduling.
>
> What if an AI agent handled all of it — and you just reviewed and approved?
>
> We build it. We manage it. $5K/month, unlimited usage.
>
> 👇 See if it fits your firm.

**Ad C: Retargeting (Visited but didn’t book)**
> You checked out AI agent setup last week.
>
> Still curious? Book a free 15-min call. We’ll audit one painful workflow and show you exactly how an agent would handle it.
>
> No pitch. Just a clear plan.

## Phase 4: Facebook / Meta Ads (Secondary Channel)

Facebook is cheaper but noisier. Use it for:
1. **Retargeting** (warm traffic from LinkedIn or your site).
2. **Lookalike audiences** (once you have 50+ bookings).
3. **Broad targeting** with interest stacks (Entrepreneurship, Real estate investing, Law practice management, Marketing agency owner).

### Campaign Structure

**Campaign 1: Retargeting**
- **Audience:** Website visitors (last 30 days), video viewers (95%), engaged shoppers.
- **Objective:** Conversions.
- **Format:** Carousel ad showing 3 use cases (Law → Marketing → Real Estate).
- **Budget:** $15–25/day.

**Campaign 2: Cold Interest Targeting**
- **Audience:** Interests = Artificial intelligence, Entrepreneurship, Small business owner. Age 30–60.
- **Objective:** Conversions or Lead generation (Meta Lead Forms — lower friction).
- **Budget:** $20–30/day.
- **Note:** This is lower intent than LinkedIn. Expect higher CAC. Use it to feed retargeting.

### Meta Ad Copy Templates

**Ad A: The Simple Hook**
> Tired of tools that promise to save time but just create more work?
>
> We do the opposite: we install a digital employee that actually works — and we manage it for you.
>
> $5K/month. Unlimited everything.
>
> 👇 Book a free 15-min call.

**Ad B: Video View → Retarget**
> [15-sec video of agent working]
> Caption: This is what a $5K/month digital employee looks like. No training. No management. Just results.
>
> Book a free call to see what yours would do.

## Phase 5: Organic LinkedIn (The Secret Weapon)

Paid ads amplify. Organic content builds trust. Nick from the video said it: *content is overpowered in 2026.*

### Content Themes (Post 3–4x/week)

1. **The Setup Loom** — 60-sec screen recordings of you configuring an agent. No face needed.
2. **The Before/After** — “Client was spending 20 hours on email follow-up. Now the agent does it in 2.”
3. **The Hot Take** — “OpenClaw is commoditized. The real value isn’t the tool. It’s the operator.”
4. **The Behind-the-Scenes** — “It’s 6 AM. My agent just finished a 4-hour task I assigned before bed.”
5. **The Invitation** — “I have 2 slots open this month for new agent setups. DM me if you run a [vertical].”

### LinkedIn Profile Optimization
- **Headline:** Not “Founder at ArwenDigital.” Try: *I install AI digital employees for busy executives. $5K/mo. First agent live in 48 hours.*
- **Featured section:** Pin your landing page + a Loom demo + a case study.
- **Banner image:** Simple text on clean background: *"Hire a Digital Employee — arnoldgamboa.com"*

## Budget Recommendations

| Phase | Timeline | Monthly Ad Spend | Expected Cost Per Call |
|-------|----------|------------------|------------------------|
| Test | Week 1–2 | $300–500 | Unknown |
| Optimize | Week 3–4 | $500–800 | $80–150 |
| Scale | Month 2+ | $1,000–2,000 | $50–100 |

**Target math:**
- 10 calls/week × $100 cost per call = $1,000/week ad spend.
- 30% close rate = 3 new clients/week.
- 3 clients × $5K = $15K MRR added per month at $4K ad spend.
- ROAS: ~3.75x (before accounting for fulfillment costs).
