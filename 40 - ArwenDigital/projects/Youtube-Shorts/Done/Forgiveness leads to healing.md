We've all been hurt by someone at some point in our lives. It's a natural part of being human. But holding onto that hurt and anger only hurts us in the end.

That's why forgiveness is so important. When we forgive someone, we're not excusing their behavior or saying that what they did was okay. We're simply choosing to let go of the anger and bitterness so that we can move forward with peace.

Forgiveness is not always easy, but it is always worth it. It's a step towards healing and freedom. 

So, if there's someone you need to forgive, I encourage you to take that step today. It doesn't mean you have to forget what happened or reconcile with the person. It simply means you're choosing to let go of the hurt and give it over to God.

Remember, forgiveness is not just something we do for others. It's something we do for ourselves so that we can experience the abundant life that God has for us.
