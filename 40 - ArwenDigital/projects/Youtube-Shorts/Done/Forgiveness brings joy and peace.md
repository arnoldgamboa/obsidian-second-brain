Want to maintain a state of peace in a broken world? Forgiveness can bring joy and peace.

Point 1:
Forgive daily. It removes the power of hurtful memories and frees us from bitterness.
Point 2:
Ask God to heal you. Trust in His power to bring healing and restoration to your heart and mind.
Point 3:
Spread peace by adding value to others. Look for ways to bring peace to others, and you will find it yourself.
Point 4:
Trust in God's promises. He promised to give us His peace, not as the world gives.
Point 5:
Remember that you are a miracle. You are fearfully and wonderfully made by God. Trust in His love and grace.
