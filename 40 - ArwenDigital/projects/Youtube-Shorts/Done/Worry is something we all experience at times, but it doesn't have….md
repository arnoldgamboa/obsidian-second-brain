1. Remember that worry is not a necessary part of life. It is possible to overcome it and find peace.
2. Use the acronym C-A-L-M to help overcome worry: Celebrate God and His Goodness, Ask God for Help, Look on the Bright Side, and Meditate on Good Things.
3. Celebrate God's sovereignty and goodness by remembering what He has done for you, even in difficult situations.
4. Pray and ask God for help with your worries. Let Him take your worries from you.
5. Focus on the good things in your life and meditate on what is true, honorable, right, pure, beautiful, and respected. This will help you feel more peaceful.
