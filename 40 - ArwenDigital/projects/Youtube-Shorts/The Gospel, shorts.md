1. Title: "The Gospel: Hope and Redemption"
Opening shot of a Bible with a cross on it.
Voiceover: "Do you ever feel like you've belittled God or failed to give Him the glory He deserves?"
Cut to a person sitting alone, looking downcast.
Voiceover: "The gospel offers hope and redemption for all of us, no matter what we've done."
Cut to footage of Christ on the cross and the resurrection.
Voiceover: "Through Christ, we have the power to overcome our struggles and deviances, no matter how impossible they may seem."
Closing shot of the Bible and cross.
Voiceover: "The gospel is the ultimate message of hope and redemption for all who believe."

1. Title: "Justification by the Cross of Christ"
Opening shot of a cross.
Voiceover: "Do you ever feel like you have to earn your way to God through your own efforts or works?"
Cut to a person trying to climb a mountain.
Voiceover: "The gospel tells us that we are justified before God by the cross of Christ alone."
Cut to footage of Christ on the cross.
Voiceover: "We don't have to try to be perfect or earn our salvation - it is a free gift given to us through faith in Christ."
Closing shot of the cross.
Voiceover: "The cross of Christ is our only hope for salvation and true righteousness."

1. Title: "The Power of the Resurrection"
Opening shot of an empty tomb.
Voiceover: "Do you ever feel like your struggles are too great to overcome?"
Cut to footage of a person struggling with addiction.
Voiceover: "The gospel tells us that the same power that raised Christ from the dead is at work in us."
Cut to footage of Christ's resurrection.
Voiceover: "Through faith in Christ, we have access to that same resurrection power."
Closing shot of the empty tomb.
Voiceover: "No matter what you're facing, remember that the power of the resurrection is available to you through faith."

1. Title: "Celebrating Christ Alone"
Opening shot of a church.
Voiceover: "Do you ever feel like you're constantly trying to prove yourself to God or others?"
Cut to footage of people in a church service.
Voiceover: "The gospel tells us that we don't have to boast in our own accomplishments or abilities."
Cut to footage of the cross.
Voiceover: "We can celebrate Christ and His sacrifice, knowing that our worth and value come from being children of God."
Closing shot of the church.
Voiceover: "So let's celebrate Christ alone, knowing that our salvation and righteousness come from Him."

Voiceover: "The gospel is not just good news - it's the best news."
