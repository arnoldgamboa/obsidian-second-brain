# YouPastor — Distribution & SEO Strategy

> Brainstormed 2026-04-30. Review this month before MVP launch.

---

## Context

- No social media following.
- SEO is the primary distribution channel.
- Target: pastors and ministry leaders searching for productivity/AI tools.
- Desktop app (Electron) with a marketing site at youpastor.com.

---

## 1. The SEO Mindset

Pastors discover tools via Google. Target searches:
- "how to write a sermon faster"
- "sermon outline template"
- "AI for pastors"
- "church email templates"
- "small group discussion questions"

These are **high-intent, low-competition** keywords. Very few SEO-focused players exist in church tech — this is a blue ocean.

---

## 2. Content Pillars (Topical Authority)

Build 4-5 clusters. Each gets a pillar page + 10-15 supporting articles.

### Pillar 1: Sermon Preparation
- Pillar: "The Complete Guide to Sermon Preparation (2026)"
- Cluster: sermon outline templates, exegesis workflow, word studies, sermon series planning, sermon illustrations, how long should a sermon be

### Pillar 2: AI for Pastors
- Pillar: "How Pastors Can Use AI Without Losing Their Voice"
- Cluster: AI sermon writing tools, ChatGPT prompts for pastors, AI for church communication, AI bible study tools, ethical AI use in ministry

### Pillar 3: Church Communication
- Pillar: "Church Communication Templates and Workflows"
- Cluster: church announcement scripts, church email templates, pastoral letters, worship leader communication, church social media calendar

### Pillar 4: Church Leadership / Productivity
- Pillar: "Productivity Systems for Pastors"
- Cluster: time blocking for pastors, meeting agenda templates, midweek devotional writing, burnout prevention, weekly workflow

### Pillar 5: Small Groups & Discipleship
- Pillar: "How to Write Small Group Discussion Guides"
- Cluster: discussion questions by book of Bible, icebreakers, discipleship pathways, small group curriculum

---

## 3. Free Tools as SEO Magnets

Highest-ROI move for zero-social distribution. Lightweight free tools that rank and funnel into YouPastor.

**Ideas:**
- Sermon Outline Generator (web-based, no signup)
- Scripture Reference Parser / Formatter
- Church Email Template Library (browse + copy)
- Small Group Discussion Question Generator
- Social Media Caption Generator for Churches
- Sermon Series Title Generator
- Greek/Hebrew Word Lookup Tool

Each tool has a soft CTA: "Want this integrated into your weekly workflow? Try YouPastor."

---

## 4. Comparison & Alternative Pages

High-converting SaaS content:
- "Best Sermon Writing Software for Pastors (2026)"
- "ChatGPT for Pastors: Is It Enough?"
- "YouPastor vs. ChatGPT for Sermon Prep"
- "Top AI Tools for Church Leaders"
- "Sermonary vs. YouPastor vs. Logos"

Write honestly about competitors. If better for a specific use case, say so.

---

## 5. Landing Pages for Every Use Case

Don't rely on homepage alone. Dedicated pages:
- `/sermon-prep-ai`
- `/church-email-templates`
- `/social-media-calendar-for-churches`
- `/small-group-discussion-guide-generator`
- `/pastor-productivity-tools`

Each targets specific keywords with clear value prop + demo/screenshot.

---

## 6. Blog Strategy

Publish **2x per week**. Actionable, specific.

**Content types:**
- Case study: "How I Prepared a Sermon on [Passage] in 2 Hours"
- Template posts: "5 Church Email Templates You Can Copy Today"
- Authority: "The Pastor's Guide to [Topic]"
- Searchable: "AI Prompts for Pastors: [Specific Use Case]"

**SEO rules:**
- Target long-tail keywords (3-5 words)
- Include "for pastors" or "for churches" in titles
- Schema markup: Article, FAQ, SoftwareApplication
- Aggressive internal linking

---

## 7. Technical SEO

Marketing site (youpastor.com) must rank even though app is desktop.

**Must-haves:**
- Load under 2s
- Mobile-first (pastors browse on phones)
- Clear CTA above fold
- Demo video or interactive demo
- Testimonials
- Pricing page (show credit-based model)
- Blog: /blog/category/post-name
- Sitemap + robots.txt
- Structured data: SoftwareApplication, Organization, BreadcrumbList

---

## 8. Backlink Strategy (No Following Needed)

**Targets:**
- Church tech blogs: ChurchMag, ChurchTechToday, WorshipIdeas
- Denominational resource sites
- Seminary blogs
- Pastor podcast show notes
- Software directories: Capterra, G2, Software Advice

**Tactics:**
- Guest post on church leadership blogs
- Original research: "2026 Pastor Productivity Survey" — data gets cited
- Free resources to church resource curators
- Podcast pitch: "the pastor building AI tools for pastors"

---

## 9. Email as Distribution Backbone

SEO brings traffic. Email captures and nurtures it.

**Lead magnets:**
- "The Pastor's AI Toolkit" (PDF)
- "52 Sermon Outline Templates"
- "Church Communication Template Library"
- "Weekly Sermon Prep Checklist"

**Nurture sequence:**
- Day 1: Deliver lead magnet
- Day 3: Share top blog post
- Day 7: Share a free tool
- Day 14: Soft YouPastor intro + early access

---

## 10. YouTube SEO

Extend the existing Youtube Shorts project into long-form.

**Video ideas that rank:**
- "How to Write a Sermon Faster Using AI"
- "ChatGPT vs. Claude for Pastors"
- "My Sermon Prep Workflow (Screen Recording)"
- "Best AI Tools for Church Leaders 2026"

YouTube is the second-largest search engine. A 10-min screen recording could rank for years.

### Month 3-6
- [ ] Full content cluster build-out
- [ ] Guest posting campaign
- [ ] Original research / survey for backlinks
- [ ] YouTube long-form content

---

## 12. What NOT to Do

- Don't waste time on X/Twitter/Threads with zero following
- Don't do generic "church leadership" content — be specific (AI + sermon prep)
- Don't ignore technical SEO (slow site kills rankings)
- Don't publish thin content — every post should be the best result for that query
