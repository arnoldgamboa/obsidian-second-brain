# YouPastor Waitlist Download Email Sequence

## Context

This sequence is for people on the YouPastor waitlist who have **not downloaded the app or created a free account yet**.

Once someone downloads YouPastor and signs up for a free account, they should be removed from this waitlist sequence and moved into a separate onboarding/usage sequence.

So every email in this sequence should point toward the same action:

> Download YouPastor and create a free account.

Tone: short, snappy, a touch of fun, pastor-caring / pastor-love. Pastoral and practical, not startup-y.

## Sequence plan

### Email 1 — YouPastor is ready

- **Timing:** Day 0
- **Purpose:** Announce that the app is live.
- **Angle:** “You joined the waitlist. It’s ready now.”
- **CTA:** Download YouPastor and create a free account.

### Email 2 — Pick one thing

- **Timing:** Day 2
- **Purpose:** Remove friction and help them imagine a first use.
- **Angle:** “Don’t explore everything. Just try one real ministry task.”
- **CTA:** Download YouPastor, sign up free, and use it for one thing already on your plate.

### Email 3 — Where it fits

- **Timing:** Day 5
- **Purpose:** Give concrete reasons to download.
- **Angle:** “Before Sunday, after Sunday, and midweek too.”
- **CTA:** Download and create a free account.

### Email 4 — Tool, not replacement

- **Timing:** Day 8
- **Purpose:** Address AI hesitation before signup.
- **Angle:** “YouPastor gives you a starting point. You are still the pastor.”
- **CTA:** Download YouPastor and try it with one real task.

### Email 5 — Gentle final nudge

- **Timing:** Day 12–14
- **Purpose:** Catch people who meant to try it but forgot.
- **Angle:** “The blank page comes back every week.”
- **CTA:** Download YouPastor and create a free account today.

### Email 4 — Tool, not replacement

**Timing:** Day 8  
**Purpose:** Address AI hesitation and build trust before download/signup.  
**Subject:** You are still the pastor  
**Preview text:** YouPastor gives you a starting point. The shepherding still belongs to you.

Hi {{ contact.FIRSTNAME | default: "Pastor" }},

I want to say this clearly:

YouPastor is a tool.

It cannot pray for your church.  
It cannot know the people you shepherd.  
It cannot sit with someone in grief.  
It cannot discern what your congregation needs this week.

That still belongs to you.

But it can help with the blank page.

It can help organize messy thoughts.  
It can help turn a sermon into small group questions.  
It can help shape an announcement so it is clearer.  
It can help you get a first draft when your brain is tired.

AI can give you a starting point.

You are still the pastor.

If you have not tried it yet, download YouPastor and create a free account here:

https://youpastor.com

Try it with one real task. Edit freely. Make it yours.

With respect for the work you carry,  
Arnold
