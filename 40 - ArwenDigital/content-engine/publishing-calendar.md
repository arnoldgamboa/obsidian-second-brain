# Publishing Calendar

## Recommended frequency

Start with a sustainable, high-signal cadence:

> **Current focus — 2026-05-23:** ArwenHQ / AI Employee / agency-client-reporting content is paused until YouPastor is done. Content reports and automations should stay YouPastor-only unless Arnold explicitly reactivates ArwenHQ.

- **Bear Blog:** 1 YouPastor post/week
- **LinkedIn:** 3 YouPastor/pastoral AI posts/week
- **X/Twitter:** 5 YouPastor/pastoral AI posts/week, plus the recurring bivocational pastors X lane (8 referenced posts researched Friday and queued Monday)
- **Future Facebook Page:** replace Threads with Facebook once the Page is set up
- **Content refresh:** 1 older post/week once there are 10+ published posts
- **SEO/GSC review:** weekly

## Default weekly rhythm

**Monday**
- Suggest/create one YouPastor blog draft.
- Queue the latest Friday-created YouPastor bivocational pastors X pack to Buffer.
- Save as Obsidian draft and Bear Blog draft when available.
- Send Arnold a concise approve/edit/reject summary.

**Tuesday**
- Publish the approved YouPastor blog post.
- If Arnold has not approved the blog draft, send a reminder instead of publishing.

**Wednesday**
- Draft social posts from the published/approved YouPastor blog.
- Save the social pack for Arnold's approval.

**Thursday**
- Push approved social posts to Buffer.
- If Arnold has not approved the social pack, send a reminder instead of pushing.

**Friday**
- Research bivocational/small-church pastor topics and create the weekly YouPastor X pack.
- Save at least 8 referenced X posts for Monday Buffer queueing.
- Optional review/catch-up only beyond that. No default publishing unless Arnold explicitly requests it.

**Saturday**
- Optional light YouPastor/pastoral reflection only.

**Sunday**
- No business automation unless explicitly requested. Optional pastoral reflection only.

## Ramp-up plan

**Weeks 1–2:** approval mode only. Hermes creates drafts and sends previews.

**Weeks 3–4:** auto-schedule social posts after approval pattern is trusted; blog remains approval-based.

**Month 2+:** Hermes can auto-publish low-risk posts if Arnold explicitly approves that policy.
